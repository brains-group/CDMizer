**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This interest rate swap contract is entered into between two parties, Buyside and Sellside, on December 12, 2010. The contract is for a fixed-for-floating interest rate swap, where Buyside pays a fixed rate and Sellside pays a floating rate based on the Mexican TIIE (Tipo de Interés Interbancario) rate.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: xyz1234 (buyside-trade-id) and abc1234 (sellside-trade-id)
* Trade Date: December 12, 2010
* Trade Lot: 1
* Counterparty: Buyside and Sellside
* Party: Buyside and Sellside

**Fixed Rate Terms**

* Notional Amount: 50,000,000 MXN
* Fixed Rate: 4.427% per annum
* Calculation Period: Annually, from October 5 to October 5 of the consecutive year
* Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after October 5, commencing on October 7, 2011, and ending on October 7, 2015
* Day Count: ACT/360

**Floating Rate Terms**

* Notional Amount: 50,000,000 MXN
* Floating Rate: Mexican TIIE (Tipo de Interés Interbancario) rate
* Calculation Period: Annually, from October 5 to October 5 of the consecutive year
* Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after October 5, commencing on October 7, 2011, and ending on October 7, 2015
* Day Count: ACT/360

**Reset Dates**

* Reset Frequency: Annually
* Reset Dates: October 5 of each year, commencing on October 5, 2011, and ending on October 5, 2015
* Fixing Dates: 2 business days prior to the reset date
* Business Day Convention: Modified Following

**Payment Dates**

* Payment Frequency: Annually
* Payment Dates: 2 business days after the reset date, commencing on October 7, 2011, and ending on October 7, 2015

**Termination**

* Termination Date: December 14, 2015
* Mandatory Early Termination: Applicable
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: November 3, 2015
