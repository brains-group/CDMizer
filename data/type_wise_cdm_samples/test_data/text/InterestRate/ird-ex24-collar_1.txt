**Interest Rate Cap and Floor Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is an interest rate cap and floor swap between Party A and Party B, with Party A acting as the payer and Party B as the receiver. The trade date is April 29, 2001, and the effective date is the same as the trade date.

**Trade Details**

The trade involves a notional amount of EUR 100,000,000, with two legs: a cap and a floor. The cap leg has a strike rate of 4.427% p.a., while the floor leg has a strike rate of 4.0625% p.a. The cap and floor rates are both floating rates based on the EUR-EURIBOR-Telerate index.

**Calculation Periods**

The calculation periods for both the cap and floor legs are from October 5 to October 5 of each year, with the first calculation period starting from the effective date and ending on October 5, 2024.

**Payment Dates**

The payment dates for both the cap and floor legs are annually, two business days after October 5 of each year, starting from October 7, 2024, and ending on October 7, 2033.

**Day Count Conventions**

The day count conventions for both the cap and floor legs are ACT/360.

**Reset Dates**

The reset dates for the cap and floor legs are based on the calculation period dates, with the reset date being two business days after the calculation period start date.

**Business Day Conventions**

The business day conventions for both the cap and floor legs are MODFOLLOWING, with the business centers being GBLO and EUTA.

The counterparty information for both Party A and Party B is as follows:

* Party A: Party A is the payer, and its party reference is Party 1.
* Party B: Party B is the receiver, and its party reference is Party 2.
