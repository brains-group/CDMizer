**Commodity Option Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is for a commodity option with a trade date of 2012-11-15 and an effective date of 2013-01-01. The contract is between Party A and Party B, with Party A being the seller and Party B being the buyer.

**Trade Details**

The trade involves a commodity option with a notional amount of 100,000 barrels of oil. The option is a call option with an exercise price of USD 80.00 per barrel. The option is exercisable on the following dates: 2013-01-31, 2013-02-28, and 2013-03-31.

**Pricing and Settlement**

The option is priced based on the Brent oil price, which is the underlying commodity. The option will be settled in cash on the settlement date, which is 5 business days after the exercise date. The settlement currency is USD.

**Payment Terms**

Party A will pay Party B the difference between the Brent oil price and the exercise price on the settlement date. If the Brent oil price is above the exercise price, Party B will receive the difference. If the Brent oil price is below the exercise price, Party A will receive the difference.

**Termination**

The contract will terminate on the earliest of the following dates: the expiration date of the option, the termination date of 2013-03-30, or the occurrence of a mandatory early termination event.
