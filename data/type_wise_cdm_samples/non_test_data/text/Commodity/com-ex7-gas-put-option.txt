**Commodity Option Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is a commodity option agreement between two parties, Party A and Party B, dated April 10, 2007. The trade identifier for this contract is 1234 and 2345, respectively.

**Trade Details**

The trade date is April 10, 2007, and the trade is a commodity option contract with a contractual product of Commodity_Option. The economic terms of the contract include an effective date of June 1, 2007, with a payout of USD 6.6 per unit.

**Option Terms**

The option is a put option with an exercise style of European and an expiration date of June 30, 2007. The option is based on the price of Natural Gas-Henry Hub-NYMEX, with a quantity of 1,500,000 units.
