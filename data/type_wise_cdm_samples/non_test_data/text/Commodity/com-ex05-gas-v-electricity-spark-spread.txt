**Underlying Exposure**

* The contract has been entered into to hedge the risk arising out of the underlying exposure of Party B, which is a USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan maturing on or after October 5, 2033.

**Purpose of the Transaction**

* The purpose of the commodity swap contract is to convert the fixed rate USD liability of Party B into a floating rate liability with the view that future commodity prices will be lower than currently projected.
