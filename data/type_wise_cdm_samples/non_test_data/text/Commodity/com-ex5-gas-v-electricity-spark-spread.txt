**Commodity Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This commodity swap contract is entered into between Party A (Bank) and Party B (Energy Co.) on June 11, 2008. The contract is subject to the terms of the ISDA Master Agreement and the Confirmation.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: 10fb089c (Party A) and f0466d0f (Party B)
* Trade Date: June 11, 2008
* Trade Lot: Two separate trade lots are defined, each with a different commodity and pricing terms.

**Commodity Swap Terms**

* Commodity: Natural Gas (Trade Lot 1) and Electricity (Trade Lot 2)
* Pricing Terms:
	+ Trade Lot 1: USD SOFR-COMPOUND
	+ Trade Lot 2: Mean of High and Low
* Calculation Period: Annually, from October 5 to October 5 of the consecutive year
* Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after October 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2008, and ending on October 7, 2033
* Day Count: A/360, Adjusted

**Party A Payments**

* Party A pays (in USD): -1.45 on the USD Notional Amount
* Party A Calculation Period: From and including October 5 to, but excluding, October 5 of the consecutive year
* Party A Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after October 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2008, and ending on October 7, 2033

**Party B Payments**

* Party B pays (in USD): USD SOFR-COMPOUND on the USD Notional Amount
* Party B Calculation Period: From and including October 5 to, but excluding, October 5 of the consecutive year
* Party B Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after October 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2008, and ending on October 7, 2033

**Mandatory Early Termination Clause**

* The swap shall be terminated on the Mandatory Early Termination Date at the market value of the swap, as on the Mandatory Early Termination Date, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Modified Following Business Day Convention.
* On 2 business days after the Mandatory Early Termination Date, the party which is out-of-the-money shall pay to the party which is in-the-money, the Cash Settlement Amount, which shall be an amount in USD equal to the absolute value of the market value of the swap, as determined on the Mandatory Early Termination Date.
