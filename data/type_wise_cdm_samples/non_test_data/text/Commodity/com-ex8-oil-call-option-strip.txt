**Commodity Option Contract**

This contract is a commodity option agreement between two parties, Party A and Party B, dated February 5, 2008. The contract is for a commodity option with a notional amount of 100,000 MT of Gas Oil-IPE.

**General Terms**

* Trade Date: February 5, 2008
* Effective Date: August 1, 2008
* Termination Date: October 31, 2008
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: November 3, 2008
* Business Day Convention: Not Applicable
* Calculation Agent: Party A (JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., Mumbai Branch)

**Pricing and Settlement**

* The option is a call option with a strike price of 1120 USD/MT.
* The option is exercisable on August 30, 2008, September 30, 2008, and October 31, 2008.
* The option is settled in cash, with the payment made on the settlement date.
* The settlement currency is USD.
* The settlement date is 5 business days after the exercise date.

**Underlying**

* The underlying commodity is Gas Oil-IPE.
* The underlying price is the settlement price of Gas Oil-IPE on the London Stock Exchange.

**Party A Payments**

* Party A pays (in USD) 4.427% p.a. on the notional amount.
* Party A Calculation Period: The period from and including August 1, 2008, to but excluding August 1, 2009.
* Party A Payment Dates: Annually 2 business days after August 1 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2008, up to and including October 7, 2009.
