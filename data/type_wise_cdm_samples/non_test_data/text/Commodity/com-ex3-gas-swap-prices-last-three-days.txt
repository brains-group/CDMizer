**Trade Details**

* The contract is a Commodity Swap with a Commodity Payout structure.
* The Commodity is Natural Gas, as specified by the commodity identifier "NATURAL GAS-HENRY HUB-NYMEX".
* The Commodity Payout is based on the settlement price of the commodity, with Party A paying the difference between the settlement price and the fixed price of USD 9.64 per unit.
* The fixed price is paid by Party A to Party B on the payment dates, which are annually on October 7th, commencing on October 7, 2009, and ending on October 7, 2009.
* The payment dates are subject to adjustment in accordance with the Business Day Convention.

**Pricing and Settlement**

* The Commodity Payout is based on the settlement price of the commodity, which is determined by the Commodity Business Calendar.
* The Commodity Business Calendar is based on the NYMEX-NATURAL-GAS business center.
* The settlement price is determined on the pricing dates, which are the last business day of each month, commencing on January 31, 2009, and ending on December 31, 2009.
* The settlement price is then used to calculate the Commodity Payout, which is paid by Party A to Party B on the payment dates.
