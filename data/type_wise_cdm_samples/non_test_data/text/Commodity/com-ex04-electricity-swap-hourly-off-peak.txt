**Commodity Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This commodity swap contract is entered into between Party A (Bank) and Party B (Energy Co.) on July 28, 2006. The contract is governed by the ISDA Master Agreement and the ISDA Definitions.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: 1234 (Party A) and 2345 (Party B)

* Counterparty: Party A (Bank) and Party B (Energy Co.)

**Contractual Terms**

* Product: Commodity Swap
* Contractual Product: Commodity Swap Fixed Float
* Effective Date: January 1, 2009
* Termination Date: December 31, 2009
* Payout: Commodity Payout (Party A pays Party B the difference between the fixed price and the floating price)
* Floating Price: USD SOFR-COMPOUND
* Fixed Price: 4.427% p.a.
* Calculation Period: Annually, from October 5 to October 5 of the consecutive year
* Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after October 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2009
