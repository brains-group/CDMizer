**Commodity Option Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is a commodity option agreement between two parties, Company A Inc. and Company B Inc., dated April 10, 2012. The contract is governed by the ISDA Master Agreement and the ISDA Definitions.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: COA1234567 (Company A) and COB7654321 (Company B)
* Trade Date: April 10, 2012
* Trade Lot: 10000 units of commodity option
* Notional Amount: USD 10,000,000
* Effective Date: September 1, 2012
* Termination Date: September 30, 2012

**Commodity Option Terms**

* Commodity: Natural Gas-Louisiana (Henry Hub)-Gas Daily
* Option Type: Put
* Strike Price: USD 4.427% per annum
* Exercise Terms: European style, automatic exercise
* Settlement Terms: Cash settlement in USD, paid on the fifth business day after the exercise date
* Calculation Agent: Calculation Agent Independent

**Counterparty Details**

* Party A: Company A Inc.
* Party B: Company B Inc.
* Counterparty Roles: Party A is the buyer, Party B is the seller

**Calculation Periods**

* Calculation Period Frequency: Daily
* Calculation Period Dates: From and including September 1, 2012, to but excluding September 30, 2012

**Observation Dates**

* Observation Dates: Business days, all
* Observation Dates Distribution: All

**Underlier**

* Underlier: Commodity
* Underlier Address: commodity-1

**Pricing Variables**

* Pricing Variable: USD SOFR-COMPOUND
* Pricing Variable Directional Impact: A downward parallel shift in the USD SOFR-COMPOUND curve will favorably impact the market value of the transaction for Party B, while an upward parallel shift will adversely impact the market value of the transaction for Party B.

**Risk Disclosure**

The parties acknowledge that this transaction involves significant risks, including but not limited to, foreign exchange risk, price risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk. The parties represent that they have made their own independent decisions to enter into this transaction and have sufficient financial resources to bear the risks associated with it.

**Representations**

Each party represents to the other party that it has the power to enter into this transaction and to deliver any document relating to this transaction and to perform its obligations under this transaction.

**Governing Law**

This contract is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario and the laws of Canada applicable therein.
