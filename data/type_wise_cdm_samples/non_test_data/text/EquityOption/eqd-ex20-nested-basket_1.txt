**Contract Description**

This contract is a call option on a basket of two Korean stocks, Hyundai Motor Company and Kia Motors Corporation, with a strike price of EUR 32.00. The option is exercisable on any business day prior to the expiration date of September 27, 2005, and the holder has the right to purchase the underlying basket at the strike price.
