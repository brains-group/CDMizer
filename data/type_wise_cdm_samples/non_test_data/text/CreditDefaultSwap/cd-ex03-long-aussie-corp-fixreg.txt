**Credit Default Swap Contract**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between Party 1 and Party 2, entered into on December 3, 2002. The contract is based on the creditworthiness of AMP Group Holdings Limited, with a reference obligation of XS0092202836.

**General Terms**

* Trade Date: December 3, 2002
* Effective Date: December 4, 2002
* Termination Date: December 4, 2007
* Mandatory Early Termination: Applicable
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: November 3, 2007
* Notional Amount: USD 10,000,000
* Business Day Convention: Modified Following
* Calculation Agent: Calculation Agent Independent
* Governing Law: English

**Payout Terms**

* Interest Rate Payout: Party 2 pays Party 1 an interest rate of USD SOFR-COMPOUND on the Notional Amount.
* Credit Default Payout: Party 1 pays Party 2 a credit default payment if AMP Group Holdings Limited experiences a credit event.

* Credit Events: Bankruptcy, failure to pay, and restructuring.
* Default Requirement: USD 10,000,000

**Underlying Exposure**

* This contract has been entered into to hedge the risk arising out of the underlying exposure of USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan, maturing on or after December 4, 2033.

**Purpose of the Transaction**

* The purpose of this credit default swap is to convert the fixed rate USD liability of Party 2 into a floating rate liability with the view that future interest rates will be lower than currently projected.
