**Credit Default Swap Contract**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between Party 1 and Party 2, with XYZ Bank as the Issuer. The trade date is December 3, 2002, and the effective date is December 4, 2002.

**General Terms**

* The contractual product is a Credit Default Swap with a single-name credit event.
* The economic terms include an effective date, termination date, and payout structure.
* The termination date is September 6, 2000.
* The payout structure includes an interest rate payout and a credit default payout.

**Interest Rate Payout**

* The interest rate payout is paid annually on October 7th of each year, commencing on October 7, 2004.
* The interest rate is USD SOFR-Compound, compounded annually.
* The notional amount is USD 5,000,000.
* The interest rate is 4.427% per annum.

**Credit Default Payout**

* The credit default payout is paid upon a credit event, which includes restructuring.
* The credit event is triggered by a credit rating downgrade or a default by the reference entity, Tenet Healthcare Corporation.
* The reference entity is identified by its entity ID, 8G836J, and its name, Tenet Healthcare Corporation.
* The credit default payout is paid annually on October 7th of each year, commencing on October 7, 2004.

**Counterparty Information**

* Party 1 is the buyer of the CDS, and Party 2 is the seller.
* The counterparty information includes the roles of Party 1 and Party 2, as well as their party references.

**Trade Lot**

* The trade lot includes a price quantity and a quantity schedule.
* The price quantity is USD 0.01, and the quantity schedule is USD 5,000,000.

**Party Information**

* The party information includes the party IDs, names, and meta information for Party 1 and Party 2.

**Contract Details**

**Risk Disclosure**
