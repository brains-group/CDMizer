**Credit Default Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This Credit Default Swap contract is entered into between Party 1 and Party 2 on January 24, 2004. The contract is based on a basket of North American Investment Grade credits.

* Trade Date: January 24, 2004
* Effective Date: March 23, 2004
* Termination Date: March 20, 2009

**Trade Product**

* Product Type: Credit Default Swap
* Product Taxonomy: Credit Default Swap_Basket
* Economic Terms:
	+ Effective Date: March 23, 2004
	+ Termination Date: March 20, 2009
	+ Payout:
		- Interest Rate Payout: Party 2 pays Party 1 an interest rate of 0.0060% per annum on a notional amount of USD 25,000,000
		- Credit Default Payout: Party 1 pays Party 2 a cash settlement amount in the event of a credit event

**Potential Conflicts of Interest**

* Potential conflicts of interest may exist in the structure and operation of this contract and the course of the normal business activities of Party 1 and Party 2.

**Underlying Exposure**

* This contract has been entered into to hedge the risk arising out of the underlying exposure of Party 2.

**Purpose of the Transaction**

* The purpose of this credit default swap contract is to convert the fixed rate liability of Party 2 into a floating rate liability with the view that future interest rates will be lower than currently projected.
