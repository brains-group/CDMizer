**Credit Default Swap Contract**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between Party 1 and Party 2, with the purpose of hedging the credit risk of Invensys plc.

**General Terms**

* Trade Date: 2002-12-02
* Effective Date: 2002-12-03
* Termination Date: 2008-01-15
* Mandatory Early Termination: Applicable
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: 2003-11-03
* Notional Amount: EUR 10,000,000
* Business Day Convention: Modified Following
* Calculation Agent: Calculation Agent Independent
* Governing Law: English

**Payout Terms**

* The CDS pays out in the event of a credit event, which includes bankruptcy, failure to pay, and restructuring.
* The payout is based on the difference between the reference price and the recovery rate.
* The reference price is the asset price of Invensys plc, which is EUR 1.0.
* The recovery rate is the percentage of the notional amount that is recovered in the event of a credit event.

**Protection Terms**

* The protection buyer (Party 1) pays a premium to the protection seller (Party 2) in exchange for protection against credit events.
* The premium is based on the credit spread, which is the difference between the risk-free rate and the credit default swap spread.
* The credit default swap spread is 100,000,000 USD.

**Calculation Agent**

* The calculation agent is responsible for determining the credit event and calculating the payout.
* The calculation agent is Calculation Agent Independent.

**Ancillary Party**

* The ancillary party is the calculation agent, which is Calculation Agent Independent.

**Counterparty**

* The counterparty is Party 1 and Party 2.

**Trade Lot**
