**Contract Description**

This is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) contract between Party 1 and Party 2, with a trade date of 2006-12-01 and an effective date of 2006-12-02. The contract has a termination date of 2010-03-21.

**General Terms**

* The contract is a single-name CDS, with the reference entity being Company DEF.
* The notional amount is USD 10,257,000.
* The protection buyer (Party 1) pays a premium to the protection seller (Party 2) based on the credit spread of the reference entity.
* The credit spread is calculated using the SOFR-Compound rate.
* The calculation period is every 3 months, with the first payment date being 2007-03-20 and the last regular payment date being 2010-03-20.

**Payout Terms**

* The protection buyer (Party 1) pays the protection seller (Party 2) a premium based on the credit spread of the reference entity.
* The premium is calculated using the SOFR-Compound rate.
* The calculation period is every 3 months, with the first payment date being 2007-03-20 and the last regular payment date being 2010-03-20.
* The protection seller (Party 2) pays the protection buyer (Party 1) a payout if the reference entity defaults.
* The payout is calculated using the SOFR-Compound rate.

**Credit Event Terms**

* A credit event occurs if the reference entity defaults.
* The protection seller (Party 2) must notify the protection buyer (Party 1) of the credit event within 2 business days.
* The protection buyer (Party 1) has the option to accelerate the contract and receive a payout.

**Calculation Agent**

* The calculation agent is Calculation Agent Independent.
* The calculation agent is responsible for calculating the credit spread and premium payments.

**Governing Law**

* The contract is governed by English law.

**Risk Disclosure**

* The contract involves significant risks, including credit risk, interest rate risk, and liquidity risk.
* The protection buyer (Party 1) should consult with their own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors before entering into the contract.
