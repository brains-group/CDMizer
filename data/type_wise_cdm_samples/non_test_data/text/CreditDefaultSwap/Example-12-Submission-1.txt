**Credit Default Swap Contract**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between two parties, Party 1 (LEI1RPT0001) and Party 2 (LEI2CP0002). The contract is for a period of 11 years, from April 1, 2018, to March 20, 2029.

**General Terms**

* The contract is for a notional amount of USD 10,000,000.
* The trade date is April 1, 2018.
* The effective date is April 1, 2018.
* The termination date is March 20, 2029.
* The business day convention is Modified Following.
* The fixing business days are US Government Bonds Market.
* The business days are New York.
* The calculation agent is Party 1 (JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., Mumbai Branch).
* The governing law is English.
* The documentation is ISDA.

**Trade Details**

* The trade is a credit default swap with a credit default payout.
* The credit default payout is based on the Dow Jones CDX NA IG.2 index.
* The index series is 2.
* The index annex date is July 6, 2004.
* The index annex source is Publisher.
* The index factor is 1.0.
* The seniority is Senior Sec.

**Payout Terms**

* Party 1 pays (in USD): 4.427% p.a. on the USD Notional Amount.
* Party 2 pays (in USD): USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on USD Notional Amount.
* The payout is based on the quantity schedule and price schedule.

**Transfer History**

* There is one transfer history entry, which is a cash transfer of USD 16,000 from Party 1 to Party 2 on an unspecified date.
