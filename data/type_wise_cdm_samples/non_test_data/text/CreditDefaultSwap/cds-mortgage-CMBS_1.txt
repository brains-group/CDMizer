**Credit Default Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between Party A (XYZ Bank) and Party B (ABC Bank). The trade date is November 14, 2006.

**Trade Details**

The trade involves a single-name CDS referencing Merrill Lynch/Countrywide Commercial, 2006-3. The protection buyer (Party B) pays a premium to the protection seller (Party A) in exchange for protection against default by the reference entity.

**Trade Specifications**

* Effective Date: November 17, 2006
* Termination Date: July 12, 2046
* Business Day Convention: Following
* Business Centers: GBLO and USNY
* Calculation Agent: Calculation Agent Independent
* Governing Law: English

**Payout Terms**

The protection buyer (Party B) pays a premium to the protection seller (Party A) in exchange for protection against default by the reference entity. The premium is calculated based on the credit spread of the reference entity.

**Credit Event Terms**

The contract defines a credit event as a failure to pay principal, writedown, or a floating amount event.

**Floating Amount Provisions**

The contract includes provisions for floating amount events, including interest shortfall, writedown, and additional fixed payments.

**Settlement Terms**

The contract includes physical settlement terms, including a settlement period of 5 business days and an escrow provision.

**Calculation Agent**

The calculation agent is Calculation Agent Independent.

**Underlying Exposure**

The contract includes a statement that the purpose of the interest rate swap transaction is to convert the fixed rate USD liability of Party B into a floating rate liability with the view that future interest rates will be lower than currently projected.
