**Credit Default Swap Contract**

**Payment Terms**

*   **Party A Payments**: Fund pays (in USD) 4.427% p.a. on the USD Notional Amount
*   **Party A Calculation Period**: The period from and including October 5 to, but excluding, October 5 of the consecutive year
*   **Party A Payment Dates**: Annually 2 Business Days after October 5 of each year commencing on October 7, 2009, up to and including October 7, 2012
*   **Party B Payments**: Broker pays (in USD) USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on USD Notional Amount
*   **Party B Calculation Period**: The period from and including October 5 to, but excluding, October 5 of the consecutive year
*   **Party B Payment Dates**: Annually 2 Business Days after October 5 of each year commencing on October 7, 2009, up to and including October 7, 2012
