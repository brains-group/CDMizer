**Credit Default Swap Index Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap Index contract between New Bank, New York (Party A) and Massive Bank, New York (Party B). The trade date is August 18, 2021.

**Trade Details**

The contract is for a Credit Default Swap Index with a notional amount of USD 10,000,000. The trade lot consists of two price quantities: one for USD 1,000,000 and another for USD 25,000,000.

**Counterparty Information**

Party A is New Bank, New York, with a global reference of "8ce119df" and an external reference of "party1". Party B is Massive Bank, New York, with a global reference of "e57f0887" and an external reference of "party2".

**Trade Product**

The contractual product is a Credit Default Swap Index with an economic term of 4 years, starting from August 18, 2021, and ending on August 18, 2025. The payout is based on the interest rate payout and credit default payout.

**Interest Rate Payout**

The interest rate payout is based on the USD SOFR-Compound rate, with a floating rate of 4.427% per annum. The payer-receiver is Party B, and the receiver-payer is Party A.

**Credit Default Payout**

The credit default payout is based on the iBoxx EUR Corporates index, with a payer-receiver of Party A and a receiver-payer of Party B.

**Trade Lot**

The trade lot consists of two price quantities: one for USD 1,000,000 and another for USD 25,000,000.

**Transfer History**

There is one transfer history entry, with a transfer date of August 18, 2021. The transfer is for USD 16,000, with a payer-receiver of Party A and a receiver-payer of Party B.

**Contract Details**

The contract details include a documentation entry with an agreement date of July 21, 2022. The contractual parties are Party A and Party B.

**Representations**

Each party represents that they are acting for their own account and have made their own independent decisions to enter into this contract. They also represent that they are capable of assessing the merits and risks of this contract and understand and accept the terms and conditions.

**Risk Disclosure**

In conducting such business, neither New Bank, New York, nor any of its affiliates or subsidiaries is obliged to take into account the circumstances of the parties to the contract or act in a manner which is favorable to them.

**Underlying Exposure**

This contract has been entered into to hedge the risk arising out of the underlying exposure of a USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan, maturing on or after August 18, 2033.

**Purpose of the Transaction**

The purpose of the credit default swap index contract is to convert the fixed rate USD liability of Party B into a floating rate liability with the view that future interest rates will be lower than currently projected.
