**Underlying Exposure**

* This contract has been entered into to hedge the risk arising out of the underlying exposure, which is a USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan maturing on or after 05 October 2033.

**Purpose of the Transaction**

* The purpose of this credit default swap is to convert the fixed rate USD liability of Party 1 into a floating rate liability with the view that future interest rates will be lower than currently projected.
