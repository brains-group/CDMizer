**Credit Default Swap Contract**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between Party A (XYZ Bank) and Party B (ABC Bank). The contract is dated October 5, 2006, and is governed by the ISDA Master Agreement.

**General Terms**

* The contract is for a single-name CDS, referencing the credit of New Century Home Equity Loan Trust 2005-4.
* The notional amount is USD 150,000,000.
* The contract has a fixed payment leg, where Party A pays an interest rate of 4.427% per annum on the notional amount.
* The contract has a credit default payment leg, where Party B pays the difference between the notional amount and the recovery value of the reference entity in the event of a credit event.

**Credit Event Terms**

* The contract defines a credit event as a failure to pay principal, a distressed ratings downgrade, or a writedown.
* The contract also includes provisions for floating amount events, including interest shortfall and writedown.

**Calculation Agent**

* The calculation agent is CalculationAgentIndependent, with a business center in USNY.

**Termination Date**

* The contract terminates on September 25, 2035.

**Payment Dates**

* The contract has annual payment dates, commencing on October 7, 2006, and ending on October 7, 2033.

**Business Day Convention**

* The contract uses the following business day convention: Modified Following.

**Governing Law**

* The contract is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario and the laws of Canada applicable therein.

**Representations**

* Each party represents that it has the power to enter into the contract and has taken all necessary action to authorize its execution and performance.
* Each party represents that its entry into and performance of the contract does not and will not result in a breach or violation of any applicable law or regulation.

**Risk Disclosure**

* The contract carries significant risks, including credit risk, interest rate risk, and liquidity risk.
* Party B should consult its own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors about the risks associated with the contract.

**Scenario Analysis**

* The contract includes a scenario analysis, which assumes various levels of USD SOFR-COMPOUND and shows the resultant cash flow gain/loss to Party B.

**Mark to Market (MTM) Sensitivity Analysis**

* The contract includes an MTM sensitivity analysis, which shows the changes in the MTM value of the contract due to parallel shifts in the USD SOFR-COMPOUND curve.

**Level of Pricing Variables and Impact of Variables on Pricing**

* The contract includes an analysis of the primary market variables used in determining the price of the contract, including the USD 10-year SOFR-COMPOUND rate.

**Valuation Statements**

* Valuation statements will be made available to Party B on the Optimize application present on JPMorgan's online platform, J.P.Morgan Markets.

**Representations and Warranties**

* Each party represents and warrants that it has the power to enter into the contract and has taken all necessary action to authorize its execution and performance.
* Each party represents and warrants that its entry into and performance of the contract does not and will not result in a breach or violation of any applicable law or regulation.
