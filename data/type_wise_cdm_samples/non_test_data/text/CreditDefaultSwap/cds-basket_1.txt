Credit Default Swap Contract

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between Party 1 and Party 2, with Morgan Stanley Capital Services and Barclays Global Investors as the counterparties. The contract is dated January 24, 2004, and is based on the ISDA 2003 Credit Derivatives Definitions.

**General Terms**

* Trade Date: January 24, 2004
* Effective Date: March 23, 2004
* Termination Date: March 20, 2009
* Mandatory Early Termination: Applicable
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: November 3, 2008
* Notional Amount: USD 25,000,000 (two tranches of USD 12,500,000 each)

**Credit Events**

* Bankruptcy: Applicable
* Failure to Pay: Applicable
* Grace Period Extension: Applicable
* Payment Requirement: USD 1,000,000 (GBP equivalent)

**Protection Terms**

* Credit Events Notice: Notifying Party: Party 2 and Party 1
* Publicly Available Information: Standard Public Sources
* Specified Number: 2

**Payout Terms**

* Interest Rate Payout: Party 1 pays Party 2 an interest rate of USD SOFR-Compound p.a. on the Notional Amount
* Credit Default Payout: Party 2 pays Party 1 a credit default payment based on the basket of reference entities (Agrium Inc. and Tenet Healthcare Corporation)

**Basket Reference Information**

* Reference Pool: Two reference entities: Agrium Inc. and Tenet Healthcare Corporation
* Constituent Weight: Agrium Inc. (60%) and Tenet Healthcare Corporation (40%)
* Nth to Default: 1

**Settlement Terms**

* Settlement Type: Cash
* Settlement Currency: USD
* Basket Reference Information: As above

**Risk Disclosure**

* The contract carries significant risks, including credit risk, interest rate risk, and liquidity risk.
* Party 2 should consult its own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors before entering into this contract.

**Ordinary Course of Business**

* JPMorgan or its affiliates may affect transactions for their own account or for the account of their customers, without taking into account the circumstances of the parties to the contract.

**Representations**

* Each party represents that it is acting for its own account and has made its own independent decisions to enter into this contract.
* Each party represents that it is capable of assessing the merits and risks of this contract and understands and accepts the terms, conditions, and risks of this contract.

**Governing Law**

* The contract is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario and the laws of Canada applicable therein.

**Calculation Agent**

* The calculation agent is JPMorgan, unless stated otherwise in the ISDA Master Agreement.

**Documentation**

* The contract is documented in accordance with the ISDA Master Agreement and the Confirmation.

**Valuation Statements**

* Valuation statements will be made available to Party 2 on the Optimize platform, or via email or other mode of communication upon request.
