**Credit Default Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between two parties, New Bank and Massive Bank. The trade date is January 24, 2005.

**Trade Details**

The trade involves a CDS Index, specifically the Dow Jones CDX NA IG.2, with a notional amount of USD 250,000,000. The trade has a fixed payment period from March 23, 2004, to March 20, 2009.

**Payout Terms**

The payout terms are as follows:

* Interest Rate Payout: New Bank pays Massive Bank an interest rate of 4.427% per annum on the notional amount.
* Credit Default Payout: Massive Bank pays New Bank a credit default payment based on the Dow Jones CDX NA IG.2 index.

* Interest Rate Payout: Annually on October 7th, starting from October 7, 2004, to October 7, 2009.
