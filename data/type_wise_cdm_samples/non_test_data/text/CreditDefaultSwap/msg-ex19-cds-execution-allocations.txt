**Credit Default Swap Contract**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between Party 1 (Barclays Global Investors) and Party 2 (Nomura Bank Global Risk Management). The contract is based on the ISDA Master Agreement and the ISDA 1999 Credit Derivatives Definitions.

**General Terms**

* Trade Date: 2002-12-03
* Effective Date: 2002-12-04
* Termination Date: 2007-09-06
* Mandatory Early Termination: Applicable
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: 2007-09-06
* Notional Amount: USD 100,000,000
* Business Day Convention: Modified Following
* Calculation Agent: Calculation Agent Independent
* Governing Law: English

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: 10000
* Trade Date: 2002-12-03
* Tradable Product: Credit Default Swap (Single Name)
* Underlying Exposure: USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan, maturing on or after 2007-09-06
* Purpose of the Transaction: To convert the fixed rate USD liability of Party 2 into a floating rate liability with the view that future interest rates will be lower than currently projected.

**Payment Terms**

* Party 1 pays (in USD): 4.427% p.a. on the Notional Amount
* Party 1 Calculation Period: From and including 2002-12-04 to, but excluding, 2007-09-06
* Party 1 Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 2002-12-04 of each year commencing on 2002-12-06 up to and including 2007-09-06
* Party 1 Day Count: A/360, Adjusted

* Party 2 pays (in USD): USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on the Notional Amount
* Party 2 Calculation Period: From and including 2002-12-04 to, but excluding, 2007-09-06
* Party 2 Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 2002-12-04 of each year commencing on 2002-12-06 up to and including 2007-09-06
* Party 2 Day Count: A/360, Adjusted

**Scenario Analysis**

* Scenario 1: USD SOFR-COMPOUND remains unchanged over the tenor of the trade; USD Overnight SOFR Curve remains unchanged till the Mandatory Termination Date
* Scenario 2: USD SOFR-COMPOUND increases over the tenor of the trade; USD Overnight SOFR Curve is 10bps higher on the Mandatory Termination Date as compared to that on Trade Date
* Scenario 3: USD SOFR-COMPOUND decreases over the tenor of the trade; USD Overnight SOFR Curve is 10bps lower on the Mandatory Termination Date as compared to that on Trade Date
