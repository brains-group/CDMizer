**Credit Default Swap Contract**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between two parties, Fund and Broker, with a notional amount of USD 70,000,000. The contract is for a single-name credit default swap, referencing the credit of ARW, a corporate entity.

**General Terms**

* Trade Date: 2009-06-08
* Effective Date: 2009-06-09
* Termination Date: 2012-06-20
* Mandatory Early Termination: Applicable
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: 2012-06-20
* Business Day Convention: Modified Following
* Fixing Business Days: US Government Bonds Market
* Business Days: New York

**Party A (Fund) Obligations**

* Pay interest rate of USD SOFR-Compound on the notional amount
* Pay interest rate of 4.427% per annum on the notional amount

**Party B (Broker) Obligations**

* Pay interest rate of USD SOFR-Compound on the notional amount
* Pay interest rate of 0% per annum on the notional amount

**Credit Event**

* Restructuring: Applicable
* Primary Obligor: ARW
* Reference Entity: ARW
* Reference Obligation: USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan maturing on or after 2012-06-20

**Payment Terms**

* Party A Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 2009-06-09, commencing on 2009-06-20
* Party B Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 2009-06-09, commencing on 2009-06-20
* Payment Frequency: Annually
* Payment Amount: USD SOFR-Compound on the notional amount
