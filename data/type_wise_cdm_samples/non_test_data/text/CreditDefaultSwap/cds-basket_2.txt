**Credit Default Swap Contract**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between Party 1 and Party 2, with Morgan Stanley Capital Services and Barclays Global Investors as the counterparties. The contract is based on a basket of two North American Investment Grade entities: Agrium Inc. and Tenet Healthcare Corporation.

**General Terms**

* Trade Date: 2004-01-24
* Effective Date: 2004-03-23
* Termination Date: 2009-03-20
* Mandatory Early Termination: Applicable
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: 2009-03-20
* Notional Amount: USD 25,000,000 (two tranches of USD 12,500,000 each)

**Payout Terms**

* The CDS pays out in the event of a credit event, which includes bankruptcy and failure to pay.
* The payout is based on the basket of two reference entities, with Agrium Inc. constituting 60% of the basket and Tenet Healthcare Corporation constituting 40%.
* The payout is calculated as the difference between the market value of the reference entities and their par value.

**Protection Terms**

* The protection buyer (Party 2) pays a premium to the protection seller (Party 1) in exchange for protection against credit events.
* The premium is calculated based on the market value of the reference entities and the credit spread.

**Credit Event Notice**

* The credit event notice is triggered by the occurrence of a credit event, which includes bankruptcy and failure to pay.
* The notice is given by the protection buyer (Party 2) to the protection seller (Party 1).

**Obligations**

* The obligations of the parties are as follows:
	+ Party 1 (protection seller): pays the premium to Party 2 (protection buyer) in exchange for protection against credit events.
	+ Party 2 (protection buyer): pays the premium to Party 1 (protection seller) and receives protection against credit events.

**Risk Disclosure**

* The parties acknowledge that there are significant risks associated with this transaction, including credit risk, market risk, and liquidity risk.
* The parties represent that they have made their own independent decisions to enter into this transaction and have sufficient financial resources to bear the risks associated with it.

**Representations**

* Each party represents to the other party that it has the power to enter into this transaction and has taken all necessary action to authorize such execution.
* Each party represents to the other party that its entry into and performance of its obligations under this transaction does not and will not result in a breach or violation of any applicable law or regulation.

**Governing Law**

**Termination Date**

**Mandatory Early Termination Date**

**Cash Settlement Amount**

* The cash settlement amount is an amount in USD equal to the absolute value of the market value of the swap, as determined on the mandatory early termination date.
