**Credit Default Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This Credit Default Swap (CDS) contract is entered into between XYZ Bank and ABC Bank, with the following terms:

* **Trade Date**: 2002-12-03
* **Effective Date**: 2002-12-04
* **Termination Date**: 2007-09-06
* **Notional Amount**: USD 5,000,000
* **Interest Rate**: 4.427% per annum
* **Payment Frequency**: Annually, 2 business days after the 5th of October of each year, commencing on 7th October 2004 and ending on 7th October 2007
* **Day Count Convention**: Actual/360, adjusted
* **Business Day Convention**: Modified Following

**Payout Terms**

* **Payer**: ABC Bank
* **Receiver**: XYZ Bank
* **Payout Frequency**: Annually, 2 business days after the 5th of October of each year, commencing on 7th October 2004 and ending on 7th October 2007
* **Payout Amount**: USD SOFR-Compound per annum on the notional amount

**Credit Event Terms**

* **Reference Entity**: Tenet Healthcare Corporation
* **Reference Obligation**: USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan, maturing on or after 5th October 2033
* **Credit Event**: Restructuring, applicable, with a restructuring type of ModR

**Collateral Terms**

* **Independent Amount**: USD 5,000,000
* **Payer Party Reference**: ABC Bank
* **Receiver Party Reference**: XYZ Bank
* **Payment Detail**: Payment rule: percentage rule, with a payment percentage of 2% of the notional amount

**Counterparty Terms**

* **Party A**: XYZ Bank
* **Party B**: ABC Bank
* **Role**: Party A pays interest, Party B pays credit default

**Risk Disclosure**

The parties acknowledge that this contract involves significant risks, including but not limited to, foreign exchange risk, price risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk. The parties represent that they have made their own independent decisions to enter into this contract and have sufficient financial resources to bear the risks associated with it.

**Representations**

Each party represents to the other party that it has the power to enter into this contract and to deliver any document relating to this contract and to perform its obligations under this contract.

**Governing Law**

This contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Province of Ontario and the laws of Canada applicable therein.

**Calculation Agent**

The calculation agent for this contract shall be XYZ Bank.

**Documentation**

The documentation for this contract shall be in accordance with the ISDA Master Agreement and the ISDA Definitions.

**Termination**

This contract shall terminate on the termination date, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the terms of this contract.

**Mark to Market**

The parties agree to mark to market this contract on a regular basis, in accordance with the terms of this contract.
