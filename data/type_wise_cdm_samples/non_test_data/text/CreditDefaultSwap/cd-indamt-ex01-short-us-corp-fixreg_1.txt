**Credit Default Swap Contract**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between XYZ Bank and ABC Bank, entered into on December 3, 2002. The contract is for a single-name CDS, with Tenet Healthcare Corporation as the reference entity.

**General Terms**

* Trade Date: December 3, 2002
* Effective Date: December 4, 2002
* Termination Date: September 6, 2007
* Notional Amount: USD 5,000,000
* Payment Frequency: Annually
* Payment Dates: October 7, 2003, to October 7, 2007
* Day Count Convention: Actual/360

**Payout Terms**

* The CDS is a credit default swap, where the protection buyer (ABC Bank) pays a premium to the protection seller (XYZ Bank) in exchange for protection against the credit risk of the reference entity (Tenet Healthcare Corporation).
* The premium is paid annually on the payment dates specified above.
* The protection buyer (ABC Bank) will receive a payout if the reference entity defaults, which will be calculated based on the notional amount and the credit spread.

**Credit Event**

* A credit event will occur if the reference entity (Tenet Healthcare Corporation) experiences a restructuring or default.
* The credit event will trigger a payout to the protection buyer (ABC Bank).

**Calculation Agent**

* The calculation agent is XYZ Bank, which will calculate the payout amount based on the credit spread and the notional amount.

**Governing Law**

* The contract is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario and the laws of Canada applicable therein.

**Representations**

* Each party represents that it has the power to enter into this contract and to perform its obligations under this contract.
* Each party represents that it is not relying on any communication (written or oral) of the other party as investment advice or as a recommendation to enter into this contract.

**Risk Disclosure**

* The parties acknowledge that there are significant risks associated with this contract, including credit risk, liquidity risk, and market risk.
* The parties agree to consult their own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors about the risks associated with this contract.

**Ordinary Course of Business**

* In the ordinary course of their business, XYZ Bank or any of its affiliates or subsidiaries may affect transactions for their own account or for the account of their customers.
* Neither XYZ Bank nor any of its affiliates or subsidiaries is obliged to take into account the circumstances of the parties to the contract or act in a manner which is favorable to them.

**Potential Conflicts of Interest**

* Potential conflicts of interest may exist in the structure and operation of the strategy and the course of the normal business activities of XYZ Bank or any of its affiliates or subsidiaries.

**Underlying Exposure**

* The purpose of this CDS is to hedge the risk arising out of the underlying exposure of ABC Bank, which is a USD 10,000,000 fixed-rate loan maturing on or after September 6, 2007.
