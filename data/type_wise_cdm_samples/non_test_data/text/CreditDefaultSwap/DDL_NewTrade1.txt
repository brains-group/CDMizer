**Contract Description**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between two parties, Party 1 and Party 2, with a notional amount of USD 10,000,000. The contract is for a term of 5 years, commencing on 30 May 2017 and terminating on 20 March 2022.

**General Terms**

* The contract is governed by the ISDA Master Agreement and the ISDA Definitions.
* The parties to the contract are:
	+ Party 1: DTCCDEMO0321 (global reference: 50f20d5)
	+ Party 2: DTCCDEMO0456 (global reference: 50f24f8)
* The trade date is 30 May 2017.
* The effective date is 31 May 2017.
* The termination date is 20 March 2022.

**Trade Details**

* The trade is a credit default swap with a single-name reference entity, PFIZED INC. (entity ID: 21657K).
* The swap is a credit default swap with a payment obligation on the occurrence of a credit event.
* The payment obligation is USD 10,000,000.
* The calculation period is quarterly, with the first calculation period commencing on 31 May 2017 and terminating on 20 March 2022.
* The payment dates are 20 June 2017, 20 September 2017, 20 December 2017, and 20 March 2022.

**Calculation Agent**

* The calculation agent is Calculation Agent Independent.
* The calculation agent's business center is GBLO.

**Payout**

* The payout is an interest rate payout, with Party 1 paying an interest rate of 4.427% per annum on the notional amount.
* The payout is calculated quarterly, with the first payout date being 20 June 2017.
* The payment dates are 20 June 2017, 20 September 2017, 20 December 2017, and 20 March 2022.

**Credit Default Payout**

* The credit default payout is a credit default payout, with Party 2 paying an amount equal to the absolute value of the market value of the swap on the occurrence of a credit event.
* The credit default payout is calculated quarterly, with the first payout date being 20 June 2017.
* The payment dates are 20 June 2017, 20 September 2017, 20 December 2017, and 20 March 2022.

**Collateral**

* The collateral is an independent amount, with Party 1 paying an amount equal to 2% of the notional amount on each payment date.
* The collateral is calculated quarterly, with the first collateral payment date being 20 June 2017.
* The payment dates are 20 June 2017, 20 September 2017, 20 December 2017, and 20 March 2022.

**Transfer History**

* The transfer history includes a single transfer, with a quantity of USD 67,000 being transferred on 2 June 2017.

**Event Identifier**
