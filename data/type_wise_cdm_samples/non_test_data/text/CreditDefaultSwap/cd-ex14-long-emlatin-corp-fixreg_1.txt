**Credit Default Swap Contract**

This contract is a Credit Default Swap (CDS) between Party 1 and Party 2, with Party 1 as the protection buyer and Party 2 as the protection seller.

**General Terms**

* Trade Date: 2002-08-23
* Effective Date: 2002-08-28
* Termination Date: 2004-08-28
* Mandatory Early Termination: Applicable
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: 2003-11-03
* Notional Amount: USD 1,800,000 (two lots of USD 900,000)
* Business Day Convention: Modified Following
* Calculation Agent: Calculation Agent Independent
* Governing Law: English

**Payout Terms**

* Protection Buyer (Party 1) pays (in USD): 4.427% p.a. on the Notional Amount
* Protection Seller (Party 2) pays (in USD): USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on the Notional Amount
* Calculation Period: Annually from and including 2002-08-28 to, but excluding, 2002-08-28 of the consecutive year
* Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 2002-08-28 of each year commencing 2002-10-07 up to and including 2003-10-07
* Day Count: ACT/360, Adjusted

**Credit Event Terms**

* Credit Events: Bankruptcy, Failure to Pay, Obligation Acceleration, Repudiation Moratorium, Restructuring
* Default Requirement: USD 10,000,000
* Credit Event Notice: Notifying Party: Party 2, Party 1; Publicly Available Information: Standard Public Sources, Specified Number: 2

**Underlying Exposure**

* Underlying Exposure: USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan, maturing on or after 2004-08-28

**Risk Disclosure**

* There are significant risks associated with this Transaction, including foreign exchange risk, price risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk.
* Counterparties should consult their own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors about the risks associated with this Transaction.
