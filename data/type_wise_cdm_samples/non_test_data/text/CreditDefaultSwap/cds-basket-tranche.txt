**Contractual Terms**

* The contract is a credit default swap, where Party 1 pays a fixed rate of interest to Party 2, and Party 2 pays a floating rate of interest based on the credit default swap spread.

* The contract has a payout structure, where Party 1 pays a fixed rate of interest to Party 2, and Party 2 pays a floating rate of interest based on the credit default swap spread.
