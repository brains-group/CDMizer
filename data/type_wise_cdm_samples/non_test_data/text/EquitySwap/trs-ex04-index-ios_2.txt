**Equity Swap Contract**

This Equity Swap Contract is entered into between two parties, Party1 and Party2, on March 23, 2011. The contract is for a total return swap, where Party1 pays the total return of an underlying index and Party2 pays the difference between the total return of the index and a fixed rate.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: TRS-01 and total-ret-swap-01
* Trade Date: March 23, 2011
* Trade Lot: 31,000,000 USD
* Counterparty: Party1 and Party2
* Adjustment: Standard

**Underlying Index**

* Index: Equity:Swap:PriceReturnBasicPerformance:SingleIndex
* Product Identifier: IOS.FN30.650.67 and 4A845HAA3
* Return Terms: Total Return

**Payout Terms**

* Interest Rate Payout: Party1 pays 4.427% p.a. on the USD Notional Amount
* Performance Payout: Party2 pays the difference between the total return of the index and a fixed rate

**Calculation Period**

**Underlying Exposure**

* Underlying Exposure: USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan, maturing on or after October 5, 2033

**Purpose of the Transaction**

* The purpose of the equity swap transaction is to convert the fixed rate USD liability of Party2 into a floating rate liability with the view that future interest rates will be lower than currently projected.
