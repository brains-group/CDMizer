**Equity Swap Contract**

This contract is an Equity Swap between Party A and Party B, with Party A acting as the calculation agent. The trade date is September 24, 2001, and the effective date is the same as the trade date.

**General Terms**

*   The notional amount is USD 350,000.
*   The swap is a variance return swap, with the underlying index being the S&P 500 Index.
*   The swap has a fixed term of 10 years, with the final valuation date being July 21, 2011.
*   The swap is subject to a mandatory early termination clause, which allows for early termination on November 3, 2003.

**Payment Terms**

*   Party A pays a fixed rate of 4.427% per annum on the notional amount.
*   Party B pays a floating rate based on the S&P 500 Index, with the rate being the difference between the index return and the variance strike price of 950.
*   The payment dates are annually, with the first payment date being October 7, 2004.

**Calculation Agent**

*   The calculation agent is Party A, who is responsible for calculating the payment amounts and determining the final valuation date.

**Governing Law**

*   The governing law is English law.

**Risk Disclosure**

*   The swap carries significant risks, including interest rate risk, fixing risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk.
*   Party B should consult with its own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors to understand the risks associated with the swap.
