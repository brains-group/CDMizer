**Equity Swap Contract**

This contract is an equity swap agreement between two parties, Party1 and Party2, entered into on 23 March 2011. The contract is for a total return swap, where Party1 pays the total return of an underlying index, and Party2 pays the difference between the total return of the index and a fixed rate.
