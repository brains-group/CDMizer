**Total Return Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This Total Return Swap contract is entered into between Party 1 (ABCD Securities Inc.) and Party 2 (HedgeCo Capital L.L.C.) on August 18, 2021.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: TRS-01 and total-ret-swap-01
* Trade Date: August 18, 2021
* Trade Lot: The contract is for a single trade lot.

**Product Details**

* Product Type: Total Return Swap
* Underlying Security: Equity (specifically, the stock of US STEEL CORP)
* Return Terms: The return is based on the total return of the underlying security, including dividends.
* Dividend Terms: The dividend payout ratio is 1.00, and the dividend is reinvested.
* Dividend Entitlement: The dividend is entitled on the ex-date.

**Payment Terms**

* Party 1 pays (in USD): The interest rate payout is based on the floating rate of USD SOFR-Compound.
* Party 1 Calculation Period: The calculation period is from the effective date to the termination date.
* Party 1 Payment Dates: The payment dates are annually, two business days after the effective date, up to and including the termination date.
* Party 1 Day Count: The day count is ACT/360, adjusted.
* Party 2 pays (in USD): The performance payout is based on the total return of the underlying security.
* Party 2 Calculation Period: The calculation period is from the effective date to the termination date.
* Party 2 Payment Dates: The payment dates are annually, two business days after the effective date, up to and including the termination date.
* Party 2 Day Count: The day count is ACT/360, adjusted.

**Termination Date**

The termination date of the contract is August 31, 2022.

**Collateral**

The contract includes an independent amount collateral, which is paid by Party 2 to Party 1. The payment detail is as follows:

* Payment Date: January 1, 2005
* Payment Amount: USD 1,000
