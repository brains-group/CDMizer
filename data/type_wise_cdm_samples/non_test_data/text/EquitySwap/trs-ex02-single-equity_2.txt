**Total Return Swap Contract**

This contract is a Total Return Swap (TRS) between two parties, Party1 and Party2, dated October 10, 2004. The contract is for a period of two years, with the termination date being October 12, 2006.

**General Terms**

* Trade Date: October 10, 2004
* Termination Date: October 12, 2006
* Mandatory Early Termination: Not applicable
* Notional Amount: USD 37,440,000 (Party1) and USD 28,469,376 (Party2)

**Product Details**

* Product Type: Total Return Swap
* Underlying Security: Shire Pharmaceuticals Group - American Depositary Receipts (SHPGY.O)
* Return Type: Total Return
* Dividend Payout Ratio: 85%
* Dividend Reinvestment: Not applicable
* Dividend Entitlement: Ex-Date
* Dividend Amount Type: Record Amount
* Extraordinary Dividends Party: Party1

**Payment Terms**

* Party1 pays (in USD): 4.427% p.a. on the USD Notional Amount
* Party1 Calculation Period: From and including October 5 to, but excluding, October 5 of the consecutive year
* Party1 Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after October 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2004, up to and including October 7, 2006
* Party1 Day Count: ACT/360, adjusted

* Party2 pays (in USD): USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on USD Notional Amount
* Party2 Calculation Period: From and including October 5 to, but excluding, October 5 of the consecutive year
* Party2 Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after October 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2004, up to and including October 7, 2006
