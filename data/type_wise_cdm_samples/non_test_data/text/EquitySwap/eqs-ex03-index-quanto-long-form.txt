**Equity Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This Equity Swap Contract is entered into between Party A (Party B) and Party B (Party A) on Trade Date: 2002-07-19. The contract is governed by the ISDA Master Agreement and the Confirmation.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: 33f59567, 33f59568
* Trade Date: 2002-07-19
* Effective Date: 2002-07-19
* Termination Date: 2004-07-21
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: Not Applicable

**Trade Lot**

The contract is a basket of four underlying indices: France CAC 40 Index, IBEX 35, Hong Kong Hang Seng Index, and USD LIBOR-BBA.

* France CAC 40 Index: 960 Index Units
* IBEX 35: 260 Index Units
* Hong Kong Hang Seng Index: 580 Index Units
* USD LIBOR-BBA: -0.00220 per USD

**Calculation Agent**

The Calculation Agent is Calculation Agent Independent.

**Governing Law**

The contract is governed by the laws of England.

**Payment Terms**

* Party A pays (in USD): 4.427% p.a. on the USD Notional Amount
* Party A Calculation Period: From and including 5th October to, but excluding, 5th October of the consecutive year
* Party A Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 5th October of each year commencing on 7th October 2024 up to and including 7th October 2033
* Party B pays (in USD): USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on USD Notional Amount
* Party B Calculation Period: From and including 5th October to, but excluding, 5th October of the consecutive year
* Party B Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 5th October of each year commencing on 7th October 2024 up to and including 7th October 2033

**Day Count**

* Party A Day Count: A/360, Adjusted
* Party B Day Count: A/360, Adjusted
