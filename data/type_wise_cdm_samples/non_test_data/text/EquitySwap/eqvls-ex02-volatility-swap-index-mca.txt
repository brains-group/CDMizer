**Equity Swap Contract**

This contract is an Equity Swap between Party A (Party1) and Party B (Party2), effective as of the Trade Date of 2015-03-30.

**General Terms**

* The Notional Amount is GBP 1000.00.
* The Trade Lot consists of one price quantity with a quantity of 1000.00 GBP.
* The observable is the FTSE index, identified by the RIC code.FTSE.
* The calculation period for Party A payments is from and including the Effective Date to, but excluding, the 5th October of the consecutive year.
* The calculation period for Party B payments is from and including the Effective Date to, but excluding, the 5th October of the consecutive year.
* The payment dates for both parties are annually 2 business days after the 5th October of each year, commencing on 7th October 2024 and ending on 7th October 2033.
* The day count convention for both parties is A/360, adjusted.

**Mandatory Early Termination Clause**

* The swap shall be terminated on the Mandatory Early Termination Date at the market value of the swap, as on the Mandatory Early Termination Date, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Modified Following Business Day Convention.
* On 2 business days after the Mandatory Early Termination Date, the party which is out-of-the-money shall pay to the party which is in-the-money, the Cash Settlement Amount, which shall be an amount in GBP equal to the absolute value of the market value of the swap, as determined on the Mandatory Early Termination Date.

**Risk Disclosure**

* There are significant risks associated with this Transaction, including but not limited to, foreign exchange risk, price risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk.
* Counterparties should consult their own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors about the risks associated with this Transaction.

**Underlying Exposure**

* The purpose of the Equity Swap is to hedge the risk arising out of the underlying exposure, which is a USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan, maturing on or after 05 October 2033.
