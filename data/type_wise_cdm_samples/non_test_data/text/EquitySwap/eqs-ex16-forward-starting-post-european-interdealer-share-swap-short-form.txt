**Equity Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is an equity swap between Party A (AAA Bank) and Party B (Mega Bank Ltd). The trade date is 2009-09-09, and the effective date is 2009-10-09.

The purpose of the equity swap is to convert the fixed-rate liability of Party B into a floating-rate liability with the view that future interest rates will be lower than currently projected.
