**Contract Description**

This is a contract for a total return basic performance equity swap, where Party 1 (Primary) pays Party 2 (Cross) a floating rate based on USD SOFR-Compound, and Party 2 pays Party 1 a performance-based payment based on the total return of an underlying equity security.
