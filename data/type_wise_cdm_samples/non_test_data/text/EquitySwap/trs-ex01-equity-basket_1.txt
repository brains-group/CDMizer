**Equity Swap Total Return Basic Performance Basket Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is an Equity Swap Total Return Basic Performance Basket contract between two parties, Party1 and Party2, entered into on December 12, 2004.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: TRS-02 and total-ret-swap-02
* Trade Date: December 12, 2004
* Trade Lot: The contract is for a basket of securities consisting of Telecom Italia spa and Nokya Oyj

**Contractual Product**

* Product Taxonomy: Equity:Swap:PriceReturnBasicPerformance:Basket
* Economic Terms:
	+ Effective Date: December 14, 2004
	+ Termination Date: December 14, 2006
	+ Payout:
		- Interest Rate Payout: Party1 pays Party2 an interest rate of USD SOFR-COMPOUND on the notional amount
		- Performance Payout: Party2 pays Party1 the total return of the basket of securities, calculated as the sum of the individual returns of Telecom Italia spa and Nokya Oyj

**Pricing and Payment**

* Notional Amount: EUR 197,851,517.16
* Pricing: The interest rate payout is calculated based on the USD SOFR-COMPOUND rate, while the performance payout is calculated based on the total return of the basket of securities
* Payment Dates: The payment dates are determined by the calculation period dates, which are December 14, 2004, December 14, 2005, and December 14, 2006

**Collateral**

* Independent Amount: The contract includes an independent amount of USD 1,000, payable on January 1, 2004

**Counterparty**

* Party1: ABCD Securities Inc.
* Party2: HedgeCo Capital L.L.C.
