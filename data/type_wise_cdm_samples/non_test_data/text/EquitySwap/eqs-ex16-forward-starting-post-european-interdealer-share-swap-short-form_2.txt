**Equity Swap Contract**

This contract is an equity swap agreement between Party A (AAA Bank) and Party B (Mega Bank Ltd). The trade date is September 9, 2009, and the effective date is October 9, 2009.

**General Terms**

* The contract is a total return basic performance single name equity swap.
* The underlying security is BMW (BMWG.DE, DE0005190003, BMW ORD).
* The swap is for a period of two years, with the first calculation period starting from October 9, 2009, and ending on October 9, 2011.
* The swap is subject to mandatory early termination on November 3, 2023, at the market value of the swap, as determined on the mandatory early termination date.

**Payment Terms**

* Party A pays (in EUR): 4.427% p.a. on the EUR notional amount.
* Party A payment dates: Annually 2 business days after October 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2024, up to and including October 7, 2033.
* Party A day count: ACT/360, adjusted.
* Party B pays (in EUR): EUR SOFR-Compound p.a. on the EUR notional amount.
* Party B payment dates: Annually 2 business days after October 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2024, up to and including October 7, 2033.
* Party B day count: ACT/360, adjusted.

**Calculation Periods**

* The calculation period for Party A and Party B starts from October 5 of each year and ends on October 5 of the consecutive year.
* The first calculation period is from October 9, 2009, to October 9, 2011.

**Notional Amount**

* The EUR notional amount is EUR 10,000,000.

**Business Day Convention**

* The business day convention is Modified Following.

**Governing Law**

* The governing law is English.

**Documentation**

**Counterparty**
