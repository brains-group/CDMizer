**Equity Swap Contract**

This contract is an equity swap agreement between Party A and Party B, entered into on September 24, 2001. The trade is identified by the global reference "33f59567" and external reference "party1" for Party A, and "33f59568" and "party2" for Party B.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Date: September 24, 2001
* Trade Identifier: Issued by Party A with global reference "33f59567" and external reference "party1", and by Party B with global reference "33f59568" and external reference "party2".
* Trade Lot: The contract is for a single trade lot.

**Product Details**

* Product Type: Equity Swap
* Underlying Security: The swap is based on the performance of IBM ordinary shares.
* Return Terms: The swap is a variance return swap, with a variance strike price of 85.00.
* Calculation Agent: The calculation agent is Calculation Agent Independent.

**Payment Terms**

* Party A Payments: Party A pays (in USD) 4.427% p.a. on the USD Notional Amount.
* Party B Payments: Party B pays (in USD) USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on the USD Notional Amount.
* Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after October 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2004, up to and including October 7, 2033.
* Day Count: A/360, Adjusted.
