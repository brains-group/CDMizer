**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This interest rate swap contract is entered into between two parties, Party1 and Party2, on May 14, 1991. The contract is for a period of one year, commencing on July 17, 1991, and terminating on July 17, 1992.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: MB87623 (assigned by Party1) and AA9876 (assigned by Party2)
* Trade Date: May 14, 1991
* Trade Lot: Two separate lots, each with a notional amount of CHF 25,000,000

**Counterparty Details**

* Party1: Party1 is the fixed-rate payer and is responsible for paying an interest rate of 4.00% per annum on the notional amount.
* Party2: Party2 is the floating-rate payer and is responsible for paying an interest rate based on the CHF-LIBOR-BBA rate.

**Calculation Period**

* The calculation period for both parties is from July 17, 1991, to July 17, 1992.
* The payment dates for both parties are on October 7 of each year, commencing on October 7, 1992.

**Day Count Fraction**

* The day count fraction for both parties is ACT/360.

**Discounting Method**

* The discounting method for both parties is FRA.

**Payment Dates**

* Party1 pays the floating-rate payer on the payment dates.
* Party2 pays the fixed-rate payer on the payment dates.

**Mandatory Early Termination**

* The contract may be terminated early on November 3, 1992, at the market value of the contract, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Modified Following Business Day Convention.

**Risk Disclosure**

* The parties acknowledge that there are significant risks associated with this transaction, including foreign exchange risk, price risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk.

**Representations**

* Each party represents that it has the power to enter into this contract and to perform its obligations under this contract.

**Governing Law**

* The contract is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario and the laws of Canada applicable therein.

**Calculation Agent**

* The calculation agent for this contract is Party1.

**Documentation**

* The contract is documented in accordance with the ISDA Master Agreement.

**Notional Amount**

* The notional amount for both parties is CHF 25,000,000.

**Currency**

* The currency for this contract is CHF.

**Interest Rate**

* The interest rate for Party1 is 4.00% per annum.
* The interest rate for Party2 is based on the CHF-LIBOR-BBA rate.

**Payment Frequency**

* The payment frequency for both parties is semi-annual.

**Business Day Convention**

* The business day convention for both parties is Modified Following.

**Termination Date**

* The termination date for this contract is July 17, 1992.

**Maturity Date**

* The maturity date for this contract is July 17, 1992.

**Counterparty Obligations**

* Party1 is obligated to pay the floating-rate payer on the payment dates.
* Party2 is obligated to pay the fixed-rate payer on the payment dates.

**Default Provisions**

* In the event of default, the non-defaulting party may terminate the contract and claim damages.

**Dispute Resolution**

* Any disputes arising out of this contract shall be resolved through arbitration in accordance with the rules of the International Chamber of Commerce.

**Entire Agreement**

**Amendments**
