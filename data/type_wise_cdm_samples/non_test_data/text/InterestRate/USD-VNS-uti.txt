**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This interest rate swap contract is between Party A (54930084UKLVMY22DS16) and Party B (48750084UKLVTR22DS78). The trade date is 2018-01-30.

**Trade Details**

The contract is a fixed-for-floating interest rate swap with a notional amount of USD 10,000,000. The fixed rate is 4.427% per annum, and the floating rate is USD SOFR-COMPOUND.

**Calculation Periods**

The calculation periods for both parties are annual, starting from the effective date (2018-01-30) and ending on the termination date (2020-09-02). The payment dates are two business days after the start of each calculation period.

**Day Count Conventions**

The day count conventions for both parties are A/360, adjusted.

**Mandatory Early Termination**

The contract is subject to mandatory early termination on the mandatory early termination date (2023-11-03). In the event of early termination, the party that is out-of-the-money will pay the cash settlement amount to the party that is in-the-money.

**Risk Disclosure**

The parties acknowledge that there are significant risks associated with this transaction, including foreign exchange risk, price risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk.

**Representations**

Each party represents that it has the power to enter into this transaction and has taken all necessary action to authorize such execution. The parties also represent that their entry into and performance of their obligations under this transaction does not and will not result in a breach or violation of any applicable law or regulation.

**Contract Description**

This interest rate swap contract is a fixed-for-floating interest rate swap with a notional amount of USD 10,000,000.
