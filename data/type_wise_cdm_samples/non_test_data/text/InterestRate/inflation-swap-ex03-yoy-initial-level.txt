**Interest Rate Inflation Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is an interest rate inflation swap between Party 1 and Party 2, with Party 1 acting as the fixed-rate payer and Party 2 acting as the floating-rate payer.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Date: 2003-11-15
* Effective Date: 2003-11-20
* Termination Date: 2007-11-20
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: Not applicable
* Notional Amount: EUR 1,000,000 (two lots of EUR 500,000)

**Interest Rate Payout**

* Party 1 pays a fixed rate of 4.427% per annum on the notional amount.
* Party 2 pays a floating rate based on the 3-month EUR IBOR rate plus 0.515% per annum.

**Calculation Period**

* The calculation period is from and including the effective date to but excluding the termination date.
* The first calculation period is from and including the effective date to but excluding 2004-11-20.

**Payment Dates**

* Party 1 payment dates: Annually 2 business days after 2003-11-20, commencing on 2004-11-20 and ending on 2007-11-20.
* Party 2 payment dates: Annually 2 business days after 2003-11-20, commencing on 2004-11-20 and ending on 2007-11-20.

**Day Count**

* Party 1 day count: 30/360, adjusted.
* Party 2 day count: 30/360, adjusted.

**Inflation Rate Payout**

* Party 2 pays an inflation rate based on the USA-CPI-U index, with a 3-month lag.
* The inflation rate is compounded quarterly.

**Reset Dates**

* The reset dates are based on the calculation period dates, with a 2-business-day delay.
* The reset frequency is quarterly.

**Compounding Method**

* The compounding method is none.

**Calculation Agent**

* The calculation agent is Party 1.

**Governing Law**

* The governing law is the law of the jurisdiction where the parties are located.

**Risk Disclosure**

* The parties acknowledge that there are significant risks associated with this transaction, including interest rate risk, inflation risk, and credit risk.

**Representations**

* Each party represents that it has the power to enter into this transaction and has made its own independent decisions to enter into this transaction.

**Acceptance**

* This contract is accepted by Party 1 and Party 2 on the date of execution.
