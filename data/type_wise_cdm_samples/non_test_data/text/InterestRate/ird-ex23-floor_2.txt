**Interest Rate Cap Floor Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is an interest rate cap floor agreement between Party A (Party1) and Party B (Party2). The trade date is 2001-04-29, and the effective date is the same as the trade date.

**Trade Details**

The trade identifier is FRG78TR45E, and the global reference is 33f59567. The trade lot consists of one price quantity, which is 100,000,000 EUR.

**Interest Rate Payout**

The interest rate payout is based on the EUR-EURIBOR-Telerate floating rate index, with a cap of 4.427% p.a. and a floor of 0%. The calculation period dates are from 2001-06-30 to 2006-06-30, with a calculation period frequency of 6 months. The payment dates are annually, 2 business days after the calculation period end date.

**Reset Dates**

The reset dates are based on the calculation period dates, with a reset frequency of 6 months. The fixing dates are 2 business days before the reset date, and the reset dates are 2 business days after the calculation period end date.

**Day Count Fraction**

The day count fraction is ACT/360.

**Counterparty**

The counterparty is Party A (Party1) and Party B (Party2).

**Party**

The party details are as follows:

* Party A (Party1): Party A is a party with the identifier "Party A".
* Party B (Party2): Party B is a party with the identifier "Party B".

**Risk Disclosure**

There are significant risks associated with this contract, including interest rate risk, fixing risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk. Party B should consult its own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors about the risks associated with this contract.

**Ordinary Course of Business**

In the ordinary course of their business, Party A or its affiliates or subsidiaries may affect transactions for their own account or for the account of their customers. This may affect the value of the contract.

**Underlying Exposure**

This contract has been entered into to hedge the risk arising out of the underlying exposure, which is a USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan, maturing on or after 05 October 2033.

**Purpose of the Transaction**

The purpose of the interest rate swap transaction is to convert the fixed rate USD liability of Party B into a floating rate liability with the view that future interest rates will be lower than currently projected.
