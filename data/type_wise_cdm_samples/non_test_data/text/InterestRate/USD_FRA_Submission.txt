**USD Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is a USD Interest Rate Swap between Party A (Michigan Avenue Bank - Executing Bank) and Party B (CMEEVENT1). The trade date is October 31, 2018, and the effective date is October 31, 2018.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: 43401180-2
* Trade Date: October 31, 2018
* Trade Lot: Two separate lots with different payment schedules and interest rates
* Counterparty: Party A (Michigan Avenue Bank - Executing Bank) and Party B (CMEEVENT1)
* Parties: Party A (Michigan Avenue Bank - Executing Bank), Party B (CMEEVENT1), and Party C (SWAPSWIRE)

**Interest Rate Terms**

* Party A pays (in USD): 4.427% p.a. on the USD Notional Amount
* Party A Calculation Period: From and including October 5 to, but excluding, October 5 of the consecutive year
* Party A Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after October 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2024, up to and including October 7, 2033
* Party A Day Count: ACT/360, Adjusted
* Party B pays (in USD): USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on USD Notional Amount
* Party B Calculation Period: From and including October 5 to, but excluding, October 5 of the consecutive year
* Party B Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after October 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2024, up to and including October 7, 2033
* Party B Day Count: ACT/360, Adjusted

**Mandatory Early Termination Clause**

This swap shall be terminated on the Mandatory Early Termination Date at the market value of the swap, as on the Mandatory Early Termination Date, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Modified Following Business Day Convention.

**Risk Disclosure**

There are significant risks associated with this Transaction, including foreign exchange risk, price risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk. Counterparties should consult their own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors about the risks associated with this Transaction.
