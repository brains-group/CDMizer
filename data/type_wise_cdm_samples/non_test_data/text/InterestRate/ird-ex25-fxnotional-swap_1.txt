**Interest Rate Cross-Currency Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is an interest rate cross-currency swap between Party A and Party B. The trade date is January 9, 2001, and the effective date is the same as the trade date.

**Trade Details**

The trade identifier is assigned by the issuer and has a global reference of "33f59567" and an external reference of "party1". The assigned identifier is "123" with a meta scheme of "http://www.partyA.com/trade-id".

The trade lot consists of two price quantities, one in JPY and the other in USD. The JPY price quantity has a value of 1,000,000,000 and a price of 0.01 per unit. The USD price quantity has a value of an observable rate option with a floating rate index of USD-LIBOR-BBA and an index tenor of 3 months.

**Counterparty**

The counterparty details are as follows:

* Party A: Party A is the fixed rate payer and has a role of "Party1".
* Party B: Party B is the floating rate payer and has a role of "Party2".

**Trade Lot**

The trade lot consists of two price quantities:

* JPY price quantity: 1,000,000,000 JPY with a price of 0.01 per unit.
* USD price quantity: an observable rate option with a floating rate index of USD-LIBOR-BBA and an index tenor of 3 months.

**Calculation Period**

The calculation period for Party A is from the effective date to the termination date, with a frequency of annually. The calculation period for Party B is also from the effective date to the termination date, with a frequency of annually.

**Payment Dates**

The payment dates for Party A are annually 2 business days after the 5th of October, commencing on October 7, 2004, and ending on October 7, 2033. The payment dates for Party B are also annually 2 business days after the 5th of October, commencing on October 7, 2004, and ending on October 7, 2033.

**Day Count**

The day count for Party A is ACT/360, adjusted. The day count for Party B is ACT/360, adjusted.

**Termination Date**

The termination date is January 11, 2011.

**Mandatory Early Termination**

The swap shall be terminated on the mandatory early termination date at the market value of the swap, as on the mandatory early termination date, subject to adjustment in accordance with the modified following business day convention.

There are significant risks associated with this transaction, including foreign exchange risk, price risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk. Counterparties should consult their own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors about the risks associated with this transaction.
