**Interest Rate Cross-Currency Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is an interest rate cross-currency swap between Party A and Party B, with Party A being the fixed-rate payer and Party B being the floating-rate payer.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Date: 2001-01-09
* Effective Date: 2006-01-11
* Termination Date: 2011-01-11
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: Not applicable
* Notional Amount: USD 10,000,000 (JPY 1,000,000,000)

**Interest Rate Terms**

* Fixed Rate: 4.427% per annum
* Floating Rate: USD SOFR-COMPOUND
* Day Count Fraction: ACT/360
* Calculation Period Frequency: 6 months
* Payment Dates: Annually 2 business days after the calculation period end date

**Floating Rate Terms**

* Floating Rate Index: USD SOFR-COMPOUND
* Index Tenor: 3 months
* Reset Frequency: 3 months
* Reset Dates: Calculation period end dates
* Fixing Dates: 2 business days prior to the reset date
* Fixing Time: 17:00:00 JPTO time
* Varying Notional Currency: USD
* Varying Notional Fixing Dates: 2 business days prior to the reset date
* FX Spot Rate Source: Bank of Japan
* Fixing Dates Adjustments: None

**Principal Payment Terms**

* Initial Payment: Yes
* Final Payment: Yes
* Intermediate Payment: Yes

**Calculation Period Dates**

* Effective Date: 2006-01-11
* Termination Date: 2011-01-11
* Calculation Period Dates Adjustments: None

**Payment Dates Adjustments**

* Business Day Convention: Modified Following
* Business Centers: GBLO, USNY, JPTO

**Reset Dates**

* ResetRelativeTo: Calculation Period Start Date
* FixingDates: 2 business days prior to the reset date
* ResetFrequency: 3 months
* ResetDatesAdjustments: None

**Counterparty Details**

* Party A: Party A
* Party B: Party B
* Counterparty Roles: Party A is the fixed-rate payer, Party B is the floating-rate payer
