**Underlying Exposure**

This Transaction has been entered to hedge the risk arising out of the underlying exposure, which is a USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan, maturing on or after October 5, 2033.

**Purpose of the Transaction**

The purpose of the interest rate swap transaction is to convert the fixed rate USD liability of Party B into a floating rate liability with the view that future interest rates will be lower than currently projected.
