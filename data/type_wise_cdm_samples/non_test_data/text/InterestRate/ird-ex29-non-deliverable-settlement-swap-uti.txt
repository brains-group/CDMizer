**General Terms**

This contract is an interest rate cross-currency swap between Party A (54930084UKLVMY22DS16) and Party B (48750084UKLVTR22DS78). The trade date is 12 December 1994.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: UITD7895394
* Trade Date: 12 December 1994
* Trade Lot: The contract is for two notional amounts, one in KRW and one in USD.

**Notional Amounts and Payment Terms**

* KRW Notional Amount: 264,150,000
* USD Notional Amount: 10,000,000
* Payment Frequency: Annually
* Payment Dates: 2 business days after 5 October of each year, commencing on 7 October 2024 and ending on 7 October 2033.
* Day Count Fraction: ACT/360
