**General Terms**

This contract is an interest rate cross-currency swap between Party A (Party1) and Party B (Party2). The trade date is December 12, 1994.

* Trade Identifier: E2000098N10184 (Party1) and 1234 (Party2)
* Trade Date: December 12, 1994

**Trade Product**

* Product Type: Interest Rate Cross-Currency Swap
* Product Taxonomy: Interest Rate Cross-Currency Fixed Float
* Economic Terms:
	+ Payer Receiver: Party1 pays to Party2
	+ Price Quantity: Party1 pays Party2 a floating rate based on the USD SOFR-Compound rate
	+ Principal Payment: Party1 pays a principal amount of $26,415,000,000 on the final payment date
	+ Settlement Terms: Settlement currency is USD
	+ Cash Settlement Terms: Cash settlement is based on the valuation method of the settlement rate option
	+ Rate Specification: The floating rate is based on the USD SOFR-Compound rate
	+ Day Count Fraction: ACT/360
	+ Calculation Period Dates: The calculation period dates are from December 16, 2004, to December 16, 2007
	+ Payment Dates: Payment dates are every 6 months, starting from October 7, 2004, and ending on October 7, 2033
	+ Reset Dates: The reset dates are based on the calculation period dates reference
