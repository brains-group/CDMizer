**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This interest rate swap contract is entered into between two parties, Party1 and Party2, on May 14, 1991. The contract is for a period of one year, commencing on July 17, 1991, and terminating on July 17, 1992.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: MB87623 (assigned by Party1) and AA9876 (assigned by Party2)
* Trade Date: May 14, 1991
* Trade Lot: Two separate lots with different payment schedules and interest rates

**Counterparty Details**

* Party1: Identified by global reference "be44d3fb" and external reference "party1"
* Party2: Identified by global reference "a887a4ca" and external reference "party2"

**Product Details**

* Product Type: Interest Rate Swap
* Product Taxonomy: Interest Rate Forward
* Economic Terms:
	+ Payout: Interest Rate Payout
	+ Interest Rate Payout: Two separate payout schedules with different interest rates and payment frequencies
	+ Day Count Fraction: ACT/360
	+ Calculation Period Dates: Effective date and termination date
	+ Payment Dates: Annually, two business days after the effective date, commencing on July 17, 1991, and terminating on July 17, 1992
	+ Payment Frequency: Six months
	+ Payment Date: Unadjusted date, with date adjustments for business days
	+ Discounting Method: FRA

**Trade Lot Details**

* Lot 1: Price Quantity: 25,000,000 CHF, with a fixed interest rate of 4.0% per annum
* Lot 2: Price Quantity: 25,000,000 CHF, with a floating interest rate based on CHF-LIBOR-BBA
