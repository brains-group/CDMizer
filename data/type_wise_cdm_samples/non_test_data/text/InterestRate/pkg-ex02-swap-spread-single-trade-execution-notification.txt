**USD Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is a USD interest rate swap between Party A (SEF Corp) and Party B (Megaclient). The trade date is January 15, 2014, and the effective date is January 17, 2014.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: 1
* Trade Date: January 15, 2014
* Notional Amount: USD 50,000,000
* Currency: USD
* Trade Type: Interest Rate Swap

**Interest Rate Swap Terms**

* Fixed Rate: 4.427% p.a.
* Floating Rate: USD SOFR-COMPOUND
* Calculation Period: Annually, from January 17, 2014, to January 17, 2019, and then every 6 months thereafter
* Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after January 5th of each year, commencing on October 7, 2014, and ending on October 7, 2023
* Day Count Fraction: ACT/360
* Reset Dates: Annually, 2 business days after January 5th of each year, commencing on October 7, 2014, and ending on October 7, 2023
* Reset Frequency: Annually
* Reset Dates Adjustments: Business Day Convention: MODFOLLOWING, Business Centers: USNY
* Calculation Period Dates Adjustments: Business Day Convention: MODFOLLOWING, Business Centers: USNY
* Payment Dates Adjustments: Business Day Convention: MODFOLLOWING, Business Centers: USNY

**Party A Payments**

* Party A pays (in USD): 4.427% p.a. on the USD Notional Amount
* Party A Calculation Period: Annually, from January 17, 2014, to January 17, 2019, and then every 6 months thereafter
* Party A Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after January 5th of each year, commencing on October 7, 2014, and ending on October 7, 2023
* Party A Day Count: ACT/360

**Party B Payments**

* Party B pays (in USD): USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on USD Notional Amount
* Party B Calculation Period: Annually, from January 17, 2014, to January 17, 2019, and then every 6 months thereafter
* Party B Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after January 5th of each year, commencing on October 7, 2014, and ending on October 7, 2023
* Party B Day Count: ACT/360

**Mandatory Early Termination Clause**

This swap shall be terminated on the Mandatory Early Termination Date at the market value of the swap, as on the Mandatory Early Termination Date, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Modified Following Business Day Convention.

**Risk Disclosure**

There are significant risks associated with this Transaction, including, but not limited to, foreign exchange risk, price risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk. Counterparties should consult their own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors about the risks associated with this Transaction.

**Representations**

Each party represents to the other party that it is acting for its own account, and it has made its own independent decisions to enter into this Transaction and as to whether this Transaction is appropriate or proper for it based upon its own judgment and upon advice from such tax, accounting, regulatory, legal, and financial advisers as it has deemed necessary.
