**Interest Rate Inflation Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is an interest rate inflation swap between Party 1 and Party 2, with Party 1 acting as the fixed-rate payer and Party 2 acting as the floating-rate payer.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Date: 2003-11-15
* Effective Date: 2003-11-20
* Termination Date: 2007-11-20
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: Not applicable
* Notional Amount: EUR 1,000,000 (two lots of EUR 500,000)

**Interest Rate Terms**

* Fixed Rate: 4.427% per annum
* Floating Rate: USD SOFR-Compound
* Calculation Period: Annually from and including 5th October to, but excluding, 5th October of the consecutive year
* Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 5th October of each year, commencing on 7th October 2004 and ending on 7th October 2007
* Day Count: A/360, Adjusted

**Inflation Terms**

* Inflation Rate: USA-CPI-U
* Index Tenor: Annually
* Inflation Lag: 3 months
* Interpolation Method: Linear Zero Yield
* Initial Index Level: 1.90%
* Fallback Bond Applicable: Yes

**Calculation Agent**

* Calculation Agent Party: Calculation Agent Independent

**Counterparty**

* Party 1: Party 1
* Party 2: Party 2

**Ancillary Party**

* Calculation Agent Independent: Party 1

**Transfer History**

* Transfer Date: 2003-11-20
* Quantity: 10,000 EUR
* Asset: Cash
* Payer Receiver: Party 1 to Party 2
* Settlement Date: 2003-11-20
* Transfer Expression: Upfront
