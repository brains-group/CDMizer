**Interest Rate Inflation Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is an interest rate inflation swap between Party1 and Party2, effective from November 15, 2003.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: E2000098N10184 (PartyA) and 1234 (PartyB)
* Trade Date: November 15, 2003
* Trade Lot: 2 lots with different payment frequencies and reset dates

**Counterparty Details**

* Party1: Party1 (payer of fixed rate)
* Party2: Party2 (payer of inflation rate)
* Counterparty: Party1 and Party2

**Trade Lot 1**

* Notional Amount: EUR 10,000,000
* Fixed Rate: 4.427% p.a.
* Payment Frequency: Annually on October 7th
* Reset Dates: November 20th (effective date) and November 20th (termination date)
* Calculation Period Frequency: Yearly
* Payment Dates: Annually on October 7th
* Day Count Fraction: 30/360
* Compounding Method: None

**Trade Lot 2**

* Notional Amount: EUR 10,000,000
* Inflation Rate: UK-RPI
* Payment Frequency: Annually on October 7th
* Reset Dates: November 20th (effective date) and November 20th (termination date)
* Calculation Period Frequency: Yearly
* Payment Dates: Annually on October 7th
* Day Count Fraction: 1/1
* Compounding Method: None
