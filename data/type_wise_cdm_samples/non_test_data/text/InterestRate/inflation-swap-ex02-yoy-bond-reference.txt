**Interest Rate Inflation Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is an interest rate inflation swap between Party 1 and Party 2, with Party 1 as the fixed-rate payer and Party 2 as the floating-rate payer.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Date: 2003-11-15
* Effective Date: 2003-11-20
* Termination Date: 2007-11-20
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: Not applicable
* Notional Amount: EUR 1,000,000 (two separate notional amounts of EUR 500,000 each)

**Fixed-Rate Terms**

* Fixed Rate: 4.427% per annum
* Fixed Rate Day Count: 30/360 Unadjusted
* Fixed Rate Calculation Period: Annually, from and including 5th October to, but excluding, 5th October of the consecutive year
* Fixed Rate Payment Dates: Annually, 2 Business Days after 5th October of each year, commencing on 7th October 2004 and ending on 7th October 2007

**Floating-Rate Terms**

* Floating Rate: USD SOFR-Compound
* Floating Rate Day Count: 30/360 Unadjusted
* Floating Rate Calculation Period: Annually, from and including 5th October to, but excluding, 5th October of the consecutive year
* Floating Rate Payment Dates: Annually, 2 Business Days after 5th October of each year, commencing on 7th October 2004 and ending on 7th October 2007
* Floating Rate Reset Dates: Quarterly, 2 Business Days after the last day of each quarter, commencing on 7th October 2004 and ending on 7th October 2007

**Inflation Terms**

* Inflation Index: USA-CPI-U
* Inflation Tenor: Annually
* Inflation Lag: 3 months
* Inflation Interpolation Method: Linear Zero Yield
