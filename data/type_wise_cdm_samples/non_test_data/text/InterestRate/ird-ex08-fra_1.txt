**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This interest rate swap contract is entered into between two parties, Party1 and Party2, on May 14, 1991. The contract is for a period of one year, commencing on July 17, 1991, and terminating on July 17, 1992.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: MB87623 (assigned by Party1) and AA9876 (assigned by Party2)
* Trade Date: May 14, 1991
* Trade Lot: Two separate lots with different payment schedules and interest rates

**Counterparty Details**

* Party1: Identified by LEI TR24TWEY5RVRQV65HD49
* Party2: Identified by LEI BFXS5XCH7N0Y05NIXW11

**Interest Rate Terms**

* Party A (Party1) pays a fixed interest rate of 4.427% per annum on a notional amount of CHF 25,000,000.
* Party B (Party2) pays a floating interest rate based on CHF-LIBOR-BBA, compounded annually, on a notional amount of CHF 25,000,000.
* The interest rate is calculated on a semi-annual basis, with payments made on July 17 and January 17 of each year.

**Calculation Period Dates**

* Effective Date: July 17, 1991
* Termination Date: July 17, 1992
* Calculation Period Dates: July 17, 1991, to July 17, 1992

**Payment Dates**
