**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This interest rate swap contract is entered into between Party A (Party1) and Party B (Party2) on the Trade Date of 2001-04-29. The contract is governed by the ISDA Master Agreement and the 2006 ISDA Definitions.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: 33f59567 and 33f59568
* Trade Date: 2001-04-29
* Trade Lot: Two separate trade lots with notional amounts of EUR 100,000,000 and EUR 100,000,000
* Counterparty: Party A (Party1) and Party B (Party2)

**Product Details**

* Product Type: Interest Rate Swap
* Contractual Product: Interest Rate IR Swap Fixed Float
* Economic Terms:
	+ Payout: Interest Rate Payout
	+ Interest Rate Payout: Two separate interest rate payout schedules with different day count fractions and calculation period dates
	+ Rate Specification: Fixed Rate and Floating Rate
	+ Fixed Rate: 4.427% per annum
	+ Floating Rate: USD SOFR-Compound
	+ Day Count Fraction: 30/360 and ACT/360
	+ Calculation Period Dates: Different calculation period dates for each party
	+ Payment Dates: Annually 2 business days after the calculation period end date
	+ Reset Dates: Different reset dates for each party

**Termination Provision**

* Mandatory Early Termination: Applicable
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: 2001-08-30
* Cash Settlement: Cash settlement in USD on the Mandatory Early Termination Date

**Risk Disclosure**

* The contract carries significant risks, including interest rate risk, fixing risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk.
* Party B should consult its own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors about the risks associated with this contract.

**Representations**

* Each party represents that it is acting for its own account and has made its own independent decisions to enter into this contract.
* Each party represents that it is capable of assessing the merits and risks of this contract and understands and accepts the terms, conditions, and risks of this contract.
