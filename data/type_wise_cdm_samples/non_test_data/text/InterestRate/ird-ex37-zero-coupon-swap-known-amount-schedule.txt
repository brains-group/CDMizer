**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

This contract is an interest rate swap between two parties, Party A and Party B. The swap is for a notional amount of EUR 10,000,000 and has a fixed rate of 4.427% p.a. for Party A and a floating rate based on EUR SOFR-Compound for Party B.

**General Terms**

* Trade Date: 2014-11-17
* Effective Date: 2014-11-17
* Termination Date: 2025-06-15
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: 2025-06-16
* Business Day Convention: Modified Following
* Fixing Business Days: US Government Bonds Market
* Business Days: New York
* Calculation Agent: Party A (unless stated otherwise in ISDA)
* Governing Law: English
* Documentation: ISDA

**Party A Payments**

* Party A pays (in EUR): 4.427% p.a. on the EUR Notional Amount
* Party A Calculation Period: The period from and including the Effective Date to, but excluding, the Termination Date
* Party A Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after the Effective Date, commencing on 2014-11-19, up to and including 2025-06-17
* Party A Day Count: ACT/360, Adjusted

**Party B Payments**

* Party B pays (in EUR): EUR SOFR-Compound p.a. on the EUR Notional Amount
* Party B Calculation Period: The period from and including the Effective Date to, but excluding, the Termination Date
* Party B Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after the Effective Date, commencing on 2014-11-19, up to and including 2025-06-17
* Party B Day Count: ACT/360, Adjusted

**Mandatory Early Termination Clause**

* The swap shall be terminated on the Mandatory Early Termination Date at the market value of the swap, as on the Mandatory Early Termination Date, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Modified Following Business Day Convention.
* On 2 Business Days after the Mandatory Early Termination Date, the party which is out-of-the-money shall pay to the party which is in-the-money, the Cash Settlement Amount, which shall be an amount in EUR equal to the absolute value of the market value of the swap, as determined on the Mandatory Early Termination Date.
