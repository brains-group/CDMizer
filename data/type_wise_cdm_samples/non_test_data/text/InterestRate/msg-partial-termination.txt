**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is an interest rate swap between two parties, Bank X and Bank Y, with Bank X acting as the fixed-rate payer and Bank Y acting as the floating-rate payer.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Date: 2011-02-12
* Effective Date: 2011-06-03
* Termination Date: 2016-02-08
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: Not applicable
* Notional Amount: USD 10,000,000
* Business Day Convention: Modified Following
* Fixing Business Days: US Government Bonds Market
* Business Days: New York
* Calculation Agent: Bank X (unless stated otherwise in ISDA)
* Governing Law: English
* Documentation: ISDA

**Interest Rate Swap Terms**

* Fixed Rate: 4.427% p.a. on the Notional Amount
* Floating Rate: USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on the Notional Amount
* Calculation Period: Annually from and including 5th October to, but excluding, 5th October of the consecutive year
* Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 5th October of each year commencing on 7th October 2024 up to and including 7th October 2033
* Day Count: A/360, Adjusted

**Mandatory Early Termination Clause**

This swap shall be terminated on the Mandatory Early Termination Date at the market value of the swap, as on the Mandatory Early Termination Date, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Modified Following Business Day Convention.

**Risk Disclosure**

There are significant risks associated with this Transaction, including foreign exchange risk, price risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk. Counterparties should consult their own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors about the risks associated with this Transaction.
