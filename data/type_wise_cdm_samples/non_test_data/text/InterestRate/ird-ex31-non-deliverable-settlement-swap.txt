**Interest Rate Cross-Currency Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is an interest rate cross-currency swap between Party A (Party1) and Party B (Party2). The trade date is December 12, 1994.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: E2000098N10184 (Party1) and 1234 (Party2)
* Trade Date: December 12, 1994
* Trade Lot: The contract is for a notional amount of 264,150,000 KRW (Party1) and 250,000,000 USD (Party2)

**Interest Rate Terms**

* Party A pays a fixed rate of 4.427% p.a. on the USD notional amount
* Party B pays a floating rate based on USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on the USD notional amount
* Calculation Period: The calculation period is from October 5 to October 5 of the consecutive year
* Payment Dates: Annually 2 business days after October 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2024, up to and including October 7, 2033
* Day Count: ACT/360

**Swap Terms**

* The swap is a cross-currency swap, with the KRW notional amount being swapped for USD
* The swap is subject to mandatory early termination on November 3, 2023, at the market value of the swap
* On 2 business days after the mandatory early termination date, the party that is out-of-the-money shall pay to the party that is in-the-money the cash settlement amount
