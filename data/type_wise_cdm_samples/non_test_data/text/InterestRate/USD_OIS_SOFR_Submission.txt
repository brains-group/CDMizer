**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

This contract is an interest rate swap agreement between two parties, Party A and Party B, with a notional amount of USD 100,000,000. The swap is for a fixed period of 6 years, commencing on November 2, 2018, and terminating on November 2, 2024.

**General Terms**

*   **Trade Date:** October 31, 2018
*   **Effective Date:** November 2, 2018
*   **Termination Date:** November 2, 2024
*   **Mandatory Early Termination:** Applicable
*   **Mandatory Early Termination Date:** November 3, 2023
*   **Business Day Convention:** Modified Following
*   **Fixing Business Days:** US Government Bonds Market
*   **Business Days:** New York
*   **Calculation Agent:** Party A (unless stated otherwise in ISDA)
*   **Governing Law:** English
*   **Documentation:** ISDA

**Party A Payments**

*   **Notional Amount:** USD 100,000,000
*   **Fixed Rate:** 4.427% per annum
*   **Calculation Period:** Annually, from October 5 to October 5 of the consecutive year
*   **Payment Dates:** Annually, 2 business days after October 5, commencing on October 7, 2024, and ending on October 7, 2023
*   **Day Count:** ACT/360, adjusted

**Party B Payments**

*   **Notional Amount:** USD 100,000,000
*   **Floating Rate:** USD SOFR-Compound
*   **Calculation Period:** Annually, from October 5 to October 5 of the consecutive year
*   **Payment Dates:** Annually, 2 business days after October 5, commencing on October 7, 2024, and ending on October 7, 2023
*   **Day Count:** ACT/360, adjusted

**Mandatory Early Termination Clause**

*   **Termination Date:** November 3, 2023
*   **Cash Settlement Amount:** The absolute value of the market value of the swap, as determined on the Mandatory Early Termination Date

**Risk Disclosure**

*   **Interest Rate Risk:** The market value of the transaction may be adversely affected if USD interest rates move from their current positions.
*   **Fixing Risk:** If USD SOFR-Compound fixings increase, the net payments made by Party B will increase.
*   **Liquidity Risk:** The transaction is not a readily liquid instrument, and there may exist a time when there is a lack of liquidity or low trading volume in the market for the transaction.
*   **Credit Risk:** The swap carries the credit risk of Party A.

**Representations**

*   **Party A:** Represents that it is acting for its own account and has made its own independent decisions to enter into this transaction.
*   **Party B:** Represents that it is entering into this transaction solely for the purpose of hedging its underlying exposure and not for purposes of speculation.
