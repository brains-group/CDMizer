**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This interest rate swap contract is entered into between two parties, Party1 and Party2, on May 14, 1991. The contract is for a period of one year, commencing on July 17, 1991, and terminating on July 17, 1992.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: MB87623 (assigned by Party1) and AA9876 (assigned by Party2)
* Trade Date: May 14, 1991
* Trade Lot: Two separate lots, each with a notional amount of CHF 25,000,000

**Counterparty Details**

* Party1: Identified by LEI TR24TWEY5RVRQV65HD49
* Party2: Identified by LEI BFXS5XCH7N0Y05NIXW11

**Interest Rate Swap Terms**

* Notional Amount: CHF 25,000,000
* Interest Rate: Party1 pays a fixed rate of 4.00% p.a. and Party2 pays a floating rate based on CHF-LIBOR-BBA
* Day Count Fraction: ACT/360
* Calculation Period Dates: Effective date: July 17, 1991, and termination date: July 17, 1992
* Payment Dates: Annually on July 17, commencing on July 17, 1992
* Business Day Convention: Following
* Business Centers: CHZU

**Floating Rate Terms**

* Floating Rate Index: CHF-LIBOR-BBA
* Index Tenor: 6 months
* Reset Dates: Fixing dates are 2 business days prior to the payment date, with a reset date of July 17, 1991

**Payment Terms**

* Party1 pays the floating rate amount to Party2 on each payment date
* Party2 pays the fixed rate amount to Party1 on each payment date

**Termination**

* The contract shall be terminated on the termination date at the market value of the swap, as on the termination date
* The party which is out-of-the-money shall pay to the party which is in-the-money, the Cash Settlement Amount, which shall be an amount in CHF equal to the absolute value of the market value of the swap, as determined on the termination date
