**Mandatory Early Termination**

* The swap shall be terminated on the mandatory early termination date at the market value of the swap, as on the mandatory early termination date, subject to adjustment in accordance with the modified following business day convention.
* On two business days after the mandatory early termination date, the party which is out-of-the-money shall pay to the party which is in-the-money, the cash settlement amount, which shall be an amount in USD equal to the absolute value of the market value of the swap, as determined on the mandatory early termination date.
