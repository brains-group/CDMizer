**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

This contract is an interest rate swap between Party 1 and Party 2, with Party 1 paying a fixed rate and Party 2 paying a floating rate based on the CAD-BA-CDOR index.

**General Terms**

* Trade Date: 2017-12-18
* Effective Date: 2020-12-14
* Termination Date: 2022-12-19
* Mandatory Early Termination: Applicable
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: 2022-12-19
* Notional Amount: CAD 634,000,000
* Business Day Convention: Modified Following
* Fixing Business Days: US Government Bonds Market
* Business Days: New York
* Calculation Agent: Party 1 (unless stated otherwise in ISDA)
* Governing Law: English
* Documentation: ISDA

**Party A Payments**

* Party A pays (in CAD): 4.427% p.a. on the Notional Amount
* Party A Calculation Period: From and including 2020-12-14 to, but excluding, 2022-12-19
* Party A Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 2020-12-14, commencing on 2021-03-15, up to and including 2022-12-19
* Party A Day Count: ACT/365.FIXED

**Party B Payments**

* Party B pays (in CAD): CAD-BA-CDOR + 0.00% p.a. on the Notional Amount
* Party B Calculation Period: From and including 2020-12-14 to, but excluding, 2022-12-19
* Party B Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 2020-12-14, commencing on 2021-03-15, up to and including 2022-12-19
* Party B Day Count: ACT/365.FIXED

**Mandatory Early Termination Clause**

This swap shall be terminated on the Mandatory Early Termination Date at the market value of the swap, as on the Mandatory Early Termination Date, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Modified Following Business Day Convention.

**Risk Disclosure**

There are significant risks associated with this Transaction, including foreign exchange risk, price risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk. Counterparties should consult their own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors about the risks associated with this Transaction.

**Interest Rate Risk**

If CAD interest rates move from their current positions, the market value of the transaction may be adversely affected from Party 2's perspective.

**Fixing Risk**

If CAD-BA-CDOR fixing increases, the NET payments made by Party 2 will increase.

**Liquidity Risk**

This Transaction is not a readily liquid instrument. There may exist a time when there is a lack of liquidity or low trading volume in the market for the Transaction, and this potential illiquidity could significantly decrease the market value of the Transaction for Party 2.

**Credit Risk**

The swap carries the credit risk of Party 1.

**Potential Conflicts of Interest**

Potential conflicts of interest may exist in the structure and operation of the strategy and the course of the normal business activities of Party 1 or any of its affiliates or subsidiaries or their respective directors, officers, employees, representatives, delegates, or agents of their normal business activities.

**Underlying Exposure**

This Transaction has been entered to hedge the risk arising out of the underlying exposure, which is a USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan, maturing on or after 2023-10-05.

**Purpose of the Transaction**

The purpose of the interest rate swap transaction is to convert the fixed rate USD liability of Party 2 into a floating rate liability with the view that future interest rates will be lower than currently projected.
