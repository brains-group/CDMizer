**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This interest rate swap contract is entered into between Bank X (Party A) and Bank Y (Party B) on April 1, 2018.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Date: April 1, 2018
* Effective Date: April 3, 2018
* Termination Date: August 30, 2026
* Notional Amount: EUR 10,000,000
* Currency: EUR

**Interest Rate Terms**

* Party A pays a fixed rate of 4.427% per annum
* Party B pays a floating rate based on EUR-EURIBOR-Telerate, compounded annually
* Calculation Period: Annually, from April 3 to April 2 of each year
* Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after April 3 of each year, commencing on April 7, 2018
* Day Count: ACT/360 for Party B payments

**Reset Dates**

* Reset Dates: Annually, 6 months after April 3 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2018
* Reset Frequency: Annually, 6 months
* Reset Business Day Convention: MODFOLLOWING
* Reset Business Centers: EUTA

**Payment Dates Adjustments**

* Payment Dates Adjustments: MODFOLLOWING
* Payment Business Centers: EUTA

**Calculation Period Dates**

* Calculation Period Dates: Annually, from April 3 to April 2 of each year
* Calculation Period Frequency: Annually, 1 period
* Calculation Period Roll Convention: 30
* Calculation Period Business Day Convention: MODFOLLOWING
* Calculation Period Business Centers: EUTA

**Calculation Period Dates Adjustments**

* Calculation Period Dates Adjustments: MODFOLLOWING
* Calculation Period Business Centers: EUTA

**Party A Payments**

* Party A pays (in EUR): 4.427% p.a. on the Notional Amount
* Party A Calculation Period: Annually, from April 3 to April 2 of each year
* Party A Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after April 3 of each year, commencing on April 7, 2018
* Party A Day Count: ACT/360

**Party B Payments**

* Party B pays (in EUR): EUR-EURIBOR-Telerate, compounded annually, on the Notional Amount
* Party B Calculation Period: Annually, from April 3 to April 2 of each year
* Party B Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after April 3 of each year, commencing on April 7, 2018
* Party B Day Count: ACT/360

**Mandatory Early Termination**

* Mandatory Early Termination Date: November 3, 2023
* Mandatory Early Termination Business Day Convention: MODFOLLOWING
* Mandatory Early Termination Business Centers: EUTA
