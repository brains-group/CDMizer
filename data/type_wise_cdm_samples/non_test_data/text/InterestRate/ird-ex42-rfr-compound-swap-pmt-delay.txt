**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This interest rate swap contract is entered into between Party A (A BANK ("ABANK")) and Party B (SELL SECURITIES CO LTD ("Counterparty")) on August 13, 2021.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: FpML-test-7
* Trade Date: August 13, 2021
* Effective Date: August 16, 2021
* Termination Date: August 16, 2024
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: November 3, 2023

**Notional Amount**

* USD Notional Amount: 3,672,000,000

**Interest Rate**

* Party A pays: 4.427% p.a. on the USD Notional Amount
* Party B pays: USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on the USD Notional Amount

**Calculation Period**

* Party A Calculation Period: From and including August 16, 2021, to but excluding August 16, 2024
* Party B Calculation Period: From and including August 16, 2021, to but excluding August 16, 2024

**Payment Dates**

* Party A Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after August 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2024, up to and including October 7, 2023
* Party B Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after August 5 of each year, commencing on October 7, 2024, up to and including October 7, 2023

**Day Count**

* Party A Day Count: ACT/360, Adjusted
* Party B Day Count: ACT/360, Adjusted

**Calculation Agent**

* Calculation Agent: Calculation Agent Independent

**Governing Law**

* Governing Law: English
