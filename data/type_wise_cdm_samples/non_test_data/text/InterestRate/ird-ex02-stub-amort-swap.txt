**Trade Product**

* Contractual Product: Interest Rate IR Swap Fixed Float
* Economic Terms:
	+ Payer Receiver: Party A pays, Party B receives

	+ Day Count Fraction: ACT/360 (Party A), 30E/360 (Party B)
	+ Calculation Period Dates: From 16 January 1995 to 14 December 1999, with a calculation period frequency of 6 months
	+ Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 16 January of each year, commencing on 14 June 1995
	+ Cashflow Representation: Cashflows match parameters, with payment calculation periods and notional amounts specified

**Underlying Exposure**
