**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This interest rate swap contract is entered into between Party A (Party1) and Party B (Party2) on the Trade Date of 1994-12-12. The contract is governed by the ISDA Master Agreement and the 2006 ISDA Definitions.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: TW9235 (assigned by Party A) and SW2000 (assigned by Party B)
* Trade Date: 1994-12-12
* Trade Lot: The contract is a single trade lot with a notional amount of USD 10,000,000.

**Counterparty Details**

* Party A: Party1
* Party B: Party2

**Trade Product**

* Product Type: Interest Rate Swap
* Contractual Product: Interest Rate Cross-Currency Fixed Float
* Economic Terms:
	+ Payout: Interest Rate Payout
	+ Principal Payment: Initial and final principal payments
	+ Rate Specification: Floating Rate with a fixed rate of 0.06 p.a. for Party B
	+ Day Count Fraction: ACT/360 for Party A and 30E/360 for Party B
	+ Calculation Period Dates: Effective date: 1994-12-14, termination date: 1999-12-14
	+ Payment Dates: Annually 2 business days after the effective date, with the first payment date on 1994-12-16
	+ Cashflow Representation: Cashflows match parameters, with payment calculation periods from 1994-12-14 to 1999-12-14

**Trade Lot Details**

* Price Quantity: USD 10,000,000 for Party A and JPY 1,000,000,000 for Party B
* Observable: Floating Rate Index: USD-LIBOR-BBA for Party A and fixed rate of 0.06 p.a. for Party B

**Mandatory Early Termination**

* Mandatory Early Termination Date: 1999-12-14
* Mandatory Early Termination Clause: The contract shall be terminated on the Mandatory Early Termination Date at the market value of the contract, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Modified Following Business Day Convention.

* Each party represents to the other party that it is capable of assessing the merits of and understanding the terms, conditions, and risks of this contract.

**Acceptance**
