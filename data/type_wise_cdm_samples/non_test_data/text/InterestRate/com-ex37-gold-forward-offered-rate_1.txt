**Interest Rate Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This interest rate swap contract is entered into between Bank A and Bank B, with Bank A acting as the fixed-rate payer and Bank B acting as the floating-rate payer.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Date: 2012-01-01
* Effective Date: 2012-01-01
* Termination Date: 2013-01-01
* Mandatory Early Termination Date: Not applicable
* Notional Amount: 100.00 XAU

**Interest Rate Terms**

* Fixed Rate: 4.427% p.a.
* Floating Rate: USD SOFR-COMPOUND
* Calculation Period: Annually, from 2012-01-01 to 2013-01-01
* Payment Dates: Annually, 2 business days after the calculation period end date
* Day Count: ACT/365.FIXED

**Reset Dates**

* Reset Frequency: Quarterly
* Reset Dates: Quarterly, 2 business days after the calculation period end date
* Fixing Dates: 2 business days prior to the reset date
* Business Day Convention: MODFOLLOWING

**Payment Terms**

* Party A (Bank A) pays the fixed rate to Party B (Bank B)
* Party B (Bank B) pays the floating rate to Party A (Bank A)

**Counterparty Details**

* Party A (Bank A): Bank A
* Party B (Bank B): Bank B

**Party Details**

* Party A (Bank A): Bank A
* Party B (Bank B): Bank B

**Risk Disclosure**

The parties acknowledge that this contract involves significant risks, including interest rate risk, fixing risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk. The parties represent that they have made their own independent decisions to enter into this contract and have sufficient financial resources to bear the risks associated with it.

**Representations**

Each party represents to the other party that it has the power to enter into this contract and to perform its obligations under it. The parties also represent that their entry into and performance of this contract does not and will not result in a breach or violation of any applicable law or regulation.

**Governing Law**

This contract is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario and the laws of Canada applicable therein.

**Calculation Agent**

The calculation agent for this contract is Bank A.

**Valuation Statements**

Valuation statements in relation to this contract will be made available to Bank B on the "Optimize" application present on Bank A's online platform, J.P.Morgan Markets.
