**Foreign Exchange Swap Contract**

**General Terms**

This Foreign Exchange Swap Contract is entered into between Party A (JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., Mumbai Branch) and Party B (Counterparty) on Trade Date: 2012-01-01.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Identifier: USITRADEID and UTITRADEID
* Trade Date: 2012-01-01
* Trade Lot: Two separate lots with different prices and quantities

**Trade Lot 1**

* Price: 1.48 (USD per GBP)
* Quantity: 10,000,000 GBP
* Currency: GBP

**Trade Lot 2**

* Price: 1.5 (USD per GBP)
* Quantity: 10,000,000 GBP
* Currency: GBP

**Counterparty**

* Party A: JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., Mumbai Branch
* Party B: Counterparty
* Counterparty Role: Party 1 and Party 2

**Payment Terms**

* Party A pays (in USD): 4.427% p.a. on the USD Notional Amount
* Party B pays (in USD): USD SOFR-COMPOUND p.a. on USD Notional Amount
* Notional Amount: USD 10,000,000
* Payment Dates: Annually 2 Business Days after 5th October of each year commencing on 7th October 2024 up to and including 7th October 2033

**Mandatory Early Termination Clause**

* This swap shall be terminated on the Mandatory Early Termination Date at the market value of the swap, as on the Mandatory Early Termination Date, subject to adjustment in accordance with the Modified Following Business Day Convention.
