**Foreign Exchange Non-Delivery Forwards (NDFs) Contract**

**General Terms**

This contract is for the purchase and sale of a foreign exchange non-delivery forward (NDF) between two parties, HSBC and BNPP. The contract is based on the ISDA Master Agreement and is subject to the terms and conditions outlined in the Confirmation.

**Trade Details**

* Trade Date: 2013-04-01
* Effective Date: Not specified
* Termination Date: Not specified
* Mandatory Early Termination: Not applicable
* Notional Amount: USD 230,700,000 (Party B) and BRL 300,000,000 (Party A)
* Business Day Convention: Modified Following
* Fixing Business Days: US Government Bonds Market
* Business Days: New York

**Counterparties**

* Party A: HSBC (payer)
* Party B: BNPP (receiver)

**Trade Lot**

* The contract is for a single trade lot with the following details:
	+ Price: 0.7690 USD/BRL
	+ Quantity: 300,000,000 BRL (Party A) and 230,700,000 USD (Party B)

**Underlying Exposure**

* The contract is entered into to hedge the risk arising out of the underlying exposure of a USD 10,000,000 fixed rate loan, maturing on or after 05 October 2033.

**Pricing Variables**

* The market level of primary market variables used in determining the price of the transaction is as follows:
	+ USD 10 year SOFR-COMPOUND Rate: 4.427%

**Risk Disclosure**

* There are significant risks associated with this transaction, including foreign exchange risk, price risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk. Counterparties should consult their own financial, legal, accounting, and tax advisors about the risks associated with this transaction.

**Representations**

* Each party represents to the other party that they are acting for their own account and have made their own independent decisions to enter into this transaction.

**Calculation Agent**

* The calculation agent for this transaction is HSBC.

**Governing Law**

* The governing law for this transaction is the law of the jurisdiction in which the parties are located.

**Documentation**

* The documentation for this transaction is the ISDA Master Agreement and the Confirmation.
