FX Swap Contract

This FX swap contract is entered into between two parties, Party A (identified by LEI 549300VBWWV6BYQOWM67) and Party B (identified by LEI 213800QILIUD4ROSUO03), on January 23, 2002. The contract is for a notional amount of GBP 10,000,000 and USD 14,800,000.

The contract is a fixed-for-floating interest rate swap, where Party A pays a fixed rate of 4.8% p.a. and Party B pays a floating rate based on USD SOFR-Compound. The floating rate is calculated on a quarterly basis, with the first payment date on October 7, 2002, and subsequent payment dates on January 7, 2003, and so on.

The contract has a tenor of 11 years, with the last payment date on October 7, 2013. The contract is subject to mandatory early termination on November 3, 2003, at the market value of the contract, with the party that is out-of-the-money paying the cash settlement amount to the other party.

The contract is governed by English law and is subject to the ISDA Master Agreement. The calculation agent is Party A, and the business day convention is modified following.

The contract has been entered into to hedge the risk arising out of an underlying exposure of a USD 10,000,000 fixed-rate loan maturing on or after October 5, 2013.
